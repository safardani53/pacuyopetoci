    In a first look at tonight's BGT, Simon, 63, sheds ALL his clothes as he indulges in a hot bubble bath.

    Wearing nothing but sunglasses, the head judge is seen holding a Union Jack rubber duck who also donned sunglasses.

    Speaking to the duck, Simon said: "I didn't like it, I loved it."

    Elsewhere in the clip, Ant and Dec are seen travelling through London towards the London Palladium in a Tuc Tuc.

    The Geordie duo looked in high spirits as they commanded attention in a yellow motorized rickshaw, nicknamed Tyneside Taxis.

    "That's Ant and Dec in the front aren't they," one bystander proclaimed.

    The video marked the second weekend of auditions for the new series.

    So far, judge Bruno Toniolli shocked viewers when he pressed his Golden Buzzer halfway through an audition, interrupting an act's performance.
