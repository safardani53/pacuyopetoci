    The American professional wrestler Nia Jax does not have a husband as she is not married. However, many years ago, she was rumored to be dating.

    Instead, Nia is giving her time to her parents and family members. Frequently, she uses her social media to show her love and affection towards her father and mother.

    Similarly, Nia’s family also has a wrestling history, as many of her relatives have been in this profession for decades.

    Despite her strong family background, Jax did not use it to climb up the ladder in the wrestling industry, as she wanted to make it on her own.

    Speaking of which, she achieved success through her dedication and utmost focus on her profession.

    After her debut in May 2015, Nia became one of the most decorated women wrestlers.

    Moreover, the wrestler is a two-time WWE Women’s Tag Team Champion and a one-time Raw Women’s Champion.

    Before starting her wrestling career, Nia was a plus-size model.

    The former model and professional wrestler also inspired many women dealing with the pressure related to their body size.

    Her positive attitude and self-love for the body has attracted many fans. As a result, Nia has 1.7 million followers on her Instagram Handle.

    In addition, the wrestler lives a quality life as she earns a massive amount via her profession. Her reported net worth is around $1 million.

    Nia Jax Husband: Is She Married Or Dating Anyone?

    The WWE wrestler, Nia, is not married yet. Also, she has no record of being married previously.

    Similarly, Jax has no man in her life as of now and is presently focused on her profession.

    Previous Relationship

    In 2014, Nia was romantically involved with Josh Woods. As per reports, they dated each other till 2016.

    Likewise, the wrestler was previously linked with Luke Gallows as well. However, none of the parties commented on the rumor.

    Insight Into Nia Jax’s Age And Parents

    Nia was born on May 29, 1984 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. The wrestler holds Gemini as her birth sign.

    The wrestler’s father, Joseph, and mother, Renate, raised her in Honolulu, Hawaii. Later on, Nia and her family migrated to San Diego, California.

    Also, the wrestler has two brothers named Patrick Fanene and Benjamine Fanene.

    Patrick is a former Corporal. Now, he has been working as the Head of Security and IT Manager at Tri-City Christian School since 2020.

    On the other hand, Nia’s younger brother, Benjamin, serves as the weightlifting coach at Army and Navy Academy. 

    Info On Her Father 

    Nia’s father, Joseph, was born in New Zealand. He is a famous actor.

    Throughout his career, he has been featured in numerous TV series, including Jake and The Fatman (1988-1990), Magnum PI (1985-1988), Island Son (1989-1990) and many more.

    Likewise, NIa’s birth father has also been indulged in many commercials, theatre productions, and films.

    Nia Jax’s Mother, Renate Fanene

    Renate has played a significant supporting role in Nia’s life. She has been a devoted mother to her children.

    Also, the wrestler regards Renate as the source of her strength.

    Nia Jax’s Education

    Nia went to the Carlsbad High School in San Diego, California.

    Then, she joined Palomar College in San Marcos, California, where she played basketball.

    After that, the professional wrestler attended Cal State San Marcos. She graduated by receiving a marketing degree from Marcos in 2005.

    Did Nia Jax Lose Her Weight?

    In October 2022, Nia began doing an intense workout with the help of Charlotte Flair, a professional wrestler.

    As a result, Nia lost 48 pounds in just six months. Before this, her recorded weight was 272 lbs (123 kg).
