    09 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Dancing with the Stars season 32 is in full swing. The brand-new season kicked off on September 26 with 14 couples. So far, each episode has ended with an elimination. This season, the show returned to using each pair’s weekly scores and America’s votes to determine who will be sent home. There is no judges’ save in season 32.

    Each week, one celebrity and their pro partner will be eliminated. This all leads up to the epic finale event where one pair will be crowned the new DWTS champion and win the Len Goodman Mirrorball Trophy. So, who’s been voted off Dancing with the Stars season 32? Scroll down to learn who has gotten eliminated so far this season.

    Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold

    Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold made a splash from the moment they hit the dance floor. They captured the hearts of viewers with their adorable partnership (and even some romance rumors). Despite not being the most skilled dancer of the bunch, Harry always had a positive attitude about dancing. Rylee dazzled in her first-ever season as a pro.

    During Taylor Swift Night, Harry and Rylee danced a rumba to “August.” They earned a 30 out of 40 from the judges. They later lost a relay dance to Alyson Hannigan and Sasha Farber. At the end of the night, Harry and Rylee were shockingly sent home.

    Barry Williams and Peta Murgatroyd

    Barry Williams and Peta Murgatroyd danced a sensual rumba to “Didn’t We Almost Have It All” during Whitney Houston Night on November 14. He earned a 32 out of 40 from the judges. During their Dance Off performance, Barry ripped open his shirt!

    Hollywood Life spoke exclusively with The Brady Bunch actor ahead of the episode about his next performance. “I don’t think it would be overstating to say that it’s the most demanding project I’ve ever been a part of in my rather lengthy career,” he admitted.

    Despite winning extra points from the Dance Off, Barry and Peta found themselves in the bottom three. Once the votes were in, Barry and Peta were sent home. After the results, the crowd chanted Barry’s name. The actor gushed over how “wonderful” the DWTS experience was for him.

    Lele Pons and Brandon Armstrong

    In yet another surprising elimination, Lele Pons and Brandon Armstrong were sent home at the end of Music Video Night. The couple had kicked off the November 7 episode with a sexy salsa to Shakira’s “Wherever, Whenever.” Lele and Brandon earned a 33 out of 40 from the judges. Along with the other Team Young’n Style members, Lele and Brandon earned a perfect score for their team dance. They were shocked just like the judges and the other cast members when they were eliminated.

    Mauricio Umansky and Emma Slater

    Mauricio Umansky and Emma Slater returned to the bottom three on Monster Night and ended up being sent home. The DWTS pair was eliminated just days after they were spotted holding hands. Mauricio and Emma fervently denied any dating rumors.

    For Monster Night, Mauricio and Emma brought the intensity with their Argentine tango and earned a 31 out of 40. Carrie Ann Inaba pointed out that Mauricio had “come so far” since his first week. But when the votes came through, it wasn’t enough to keep Mauricio and Emma in the competition.

    Mira Sorvino and Gleb Savchenko

    Mira Sorvino and Gleb Savchenko kicked off Most Memorable Year Night with a beautiful contemporary performance set to Cyndi Lauper’s “Time After Time.” The performance also featured Mira’s daughter, Mattea Backus. The judges appreciated Mira’s “heartfelt” performance, and she earned a 22 out of 30 with Gleb.

    But Mira and Gleb found themselves in the bottom three for the first time in the October 24 episode, alongside Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold, as well as Alyson Hannigan and Sasha Farber. In the “closest vote” of the season so far, Mira and Gleb were eliminated. Mira was absolutely shocked by the results and began tearing up in the ballroom.

    Adrian Peterson and Britt Stewart

    Adrian Peterson and Britt Stewart shined on Disney Night, but it wasn’t able to save their place in the competition. During the October 17 episode of DWTS, Adrian and Britt danced a beautiful Viennese waltz to “Baby Mine” from Dumbo. “You have literally transformed before our eyes,” Derek Hough told the couple.

    The pair earned their highest score yet: 25 out of 30. However, Adrian and Britt were sent home at the end of the night.

    Tyson Beckford and Jenna Johnson

    Tyson Beckford and Jenna Johnson were the third DWTS couple of season 32 to be eliminated. After making a comeback during Latin Night, Tyson stumbled with Motown Night. The model’s foxtrot with Jenna earned them a 20 out of 40.

    Prior to Motown Night, Tyson and Jenna had not been in the bottom two. The pair went up against Harry Jowsey/Rylee Arnold and Alyson Hannigan/Sasha Farber. When the judges’ scores were combined with America’s votes, Tyson and Jenna ranked last among the bottom three.

    Jamie Lynn Spears and Alan Bersten

    The competition took a turn when Jamie Lynn Spears and Alan Bersten were shockingly eliminated in the second week of season 32. During the show’s Latin Night, Jamie Lynn and Alan earned a 16 out of 30 for their cha cha. Despite being firmly in the middle of the pack, the Zoey 101 alum and her partner were sent home after facing off with Adrian Peterson and Britt Stewart in the bottom two.

    “Yalllllll, I promise I hear and see all the many wonderfully passionate [people] who are not happy with the way things fell tonight, and I appreciate the tremendous amount love and support,” Jamie Lynn wrote on Instagram after the episode. “But this was an amazing experience, and I’m very happy to have been a part of it, and soooo excited to see all the fabulous contestants and their partners continue to KILL it each week [sic]!!!!!

    Matt Walsh and Koko Iwasaki

    Matt Walsh and Koko Iwasaki were the first couple of Dancing with the Stars season 32 to be sent home. Prior to the premiere, Matt temporarily stepped away from the show in solidarity with the WGA strike, but he returned once the union reached a deal with the studios.

    Matt and Koko danced a cha cha in the season 32 premiere and earned a 12 out of 30. Unfortunately, the votes that came in for Matt and Koko weren’t enough to save the pair from elimination.
