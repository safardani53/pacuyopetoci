    Gorgeous actress Jessica Chastain is dating Gian Luca Passi de Preposulo who is a member of one of Italy’s most wealthy families. Chastain and Luca make up a great pair without any doubts. This couple is in a loving relationship for many years. However, there is not a single hint that they might tie the knot soon. Today we will be talking about Chastain and Luca’s relationship.

    Jessica Chastain and Luca Passi de Preposulo's affair

    Chastain first saw Luca during ‘Dr.Pozzi at Home’ performance. “Oh, my, look at the eyes—he’s Gian Luca!” this is exactly how Chastain exclaimed when she saw him for the first time. Later they become acquaintances through fashion circles. Soon after that they dated and fell in love. “He’s a gentleman and that’s very important to me. He’s from an old-school Italian family. No one in his family has ever been divorced!” says Chastain.

    Even though Chastain tried to hide her affair, in the beginning, it came out subsequently. Time and again, this couple is seen making a public appearance and arriving at various events and parties together. 

    You might be amazed to know that Chastain’s boyfriend Luca is a count who belongs to a long line of nobility. He is wealthy enough to live his life luxuriously without any job but he is a very wise man. He has been very successful in establishing himself as a luxury fashion magnate. In 2012, he worked as an executive for Moncler. He also worked as the Director of Public Relations at Armani before that.

    Talking about Chastain and Luca’s relationship, Luca seems to be a very supportive boyfriend. It looks like his love for Chastain is pure. During an interview in 2013, Chastain said: “It’s a wonderful thing when a career does so well and your personal life goes so well,” 

    Are Jessica Chastain and Luca Passi de Preposulo getting married?

    It looks like both Chastain and Luca wants to focus on their career at the moment. Therefore, despite being in so long relationship, neither of the pair has shown an urge to get tie the knot.

    We have to accept the fact that Luca is a very lucky man. If someday this couple tie the knot then we have to agree again that he will be one of the luckiest men, as it is an honor to be the husband of beauty like Chastain.
