    KOURTNEY Kardashian has never been ashamed of her body.

    The Kardashians star, who recently gave birth to her fourth child, has been public about many aspects and phases of her life.

    Since first being introduced to the world on Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Kourtney has transformed from pin-thin partier to curvy mom.

    But the 44-year-old has been largely inactive on social media since she welcomed her son, Rocky Thirteen, with her husband, Travis Barker, 47.

    CHANGES

    It's been a busy decade of change for the reality star, as one fan proved in an online Kardashian forum.

    The carousel of photos included the Lemme founder's Vanity Fair cover, mirror selfies, professional snaps, and paparazzi shots with her Blink-182 rocker beau.

    ROCKER CHIC

    "Her fashion evolution is so godawful it pains me," one wrote.

    IVF BODY

    Kourtney has been documenting her changing body, especially during her recent pregnancy.

    Kourtney proudly embraced the changes happening to her body as she underwent in vitro fertilization in the hopes of having a child with her husband, Travis Barker.

    In the Instagram pic, 44-year-old Kourtney wore a black string bikini as she relaxed on a large wooden deckchair with her torso on full display.

    Just above her unedited stomach, Kourtney had typed "a little IVF body."

    IVF JOURNEY

    During Season 2 of The Kardashians, Kourtney opened up about her attempts to get pregnant - including through IVF.

    “Travis and I want to have a baby, and so my doctor took us down this road of doing IVF,” Kourtney said in a confessional during one episode.

    “Like, it hasn’t been the most amazing experience," she added.

    She told mom Kris Jenner: "Every single person on social media is always like, ‘Kourtney’s pregnant, Kourtney’s pregnant, Kourtney’s gained so much weight.'

    "It’s so rude to comment on people when you have no idea what they’re actually going through. The medication they’re giving me, they put me into menopause. … Literally into menopause.”

    "Thank you so much for not editing out your stretch marks!" one person commented, eliciting a response from the star herself.

    "I love my little stripes [blush emoji]," Kourtney replied.
