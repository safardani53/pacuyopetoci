    05 Feb 2024, 00:00

    A MAN has been charged with the double murder of a husband and wife at a house inside a cemetery in East Sussex.

    Police discovered the bodies of a 33-year-old man and 30-year-old woman at the property in Lewes Road, Newhaven, at around 7pm on Friday.

    Their next of kin have been informed and are being supported by specialist officers.

    Derek Martin, also known as Derek Glenn, 64, from Brighton, Sussex, has been charged with the pair's murder in Newhaven Cemetary.
