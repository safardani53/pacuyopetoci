    Taylor Swift doesn't just wear her heart on her sleeve — she accessorizes with it, too!

    Since the pop sensation went public with her NFL star boyfriend, Travis Kelce, the world has seen her sport stylish Kansas City Chiefs merch on game days. Swift has also taken to wearing more personal pieces like bracelets beaded with his number and an earring in the shape of his first initial.

    From her more subtle bling to more overt wardrobe additions, check out all the sweet nods to her football-playing boyfriend that Taylor Swift has worn to cheer on the Chiefs.

    The '87' Santa Hat

    On Christmas Day — or should we say "Swiftmas" Day — the hitmaker upped her headwear game with a Santa Claus hat emblazoned with the number "87" on the trim — a nod to Kelce's jersey number. Swift matched with a few other Chiefs WAGs ("Wives and Girlfriends") — including Brittany Mahomes — who wore similar red-and-white caps with their partners' numbers on them.

    A T-Shaped Earring

    Eagle-eyed onlookers spotted the 12-time Grammy winner rocking a shiny T-shaped earring during the Chiefs home game against the Las Vegas Raiders, which Swift's mom, dad, brother and his girlfriend attended as well.

    The 'Trav' Bracelet

    Swift was spotted wearing Kelce's nickname around her wrist during a December holiday party. According to the "Trav" bracelet's designer, jewelry brand Erimish, the accessory was a gift from Randi Mahomes, Patrick Mahomes' mother.

    The duo behind Erimish, sisters Misha Wilson and Ericka Hamilton, told PEOPLE their Kansas City-based business has skyrocketed since Swift wore their bling.

    "We have garnered an entirely new following and customer base," Wilson said. "We're so excited! We always had products for all ages, but this has really put a spotlight on how diverse our selection is."

    The Crocheted '87' Beanie

    The "Cruel Summer" singer cozied up in a red crocheted beanie to match the Chiefs' color during their matchup against the New England Patriots on Dec. 18. Embroidered with Kelce's number in fine black yarn, the handmade hat was sourced from Depop seller @kuttheknit. Unsurprisingly, after Swift sported it at a December game, the item is now sold out.

    An '87' Bracelet

    In one of her earliest nods to the Super Bowl champion, the singer slipped on what could be considered a more-than-friendship bracelet beaded with the number 87 and two hearts. She first sported the gold Erimish wristwear at a Chiefs game in October.

    Beyond the subtle support and team spirit, the accessory also called back to what brought the singer and Kelce together in the first place. In July, the athlete claimed he tried to give Swift a friendship bracelet with his phone number on it, but he didn't get the chance to meet her after her Eras Tour concert.

    In her Time Person of the Year cover story months later, the "Anti-Hero" singer revealed that the gesture did, in fact, launch their relationship.
