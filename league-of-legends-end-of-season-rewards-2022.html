    League of Legends end of season rewards announced All season rewards for 2022

    LEAGUE of Legends latest season is coming to an end, and all the seasons rewards have now been revealed to players. Despite the announcement of the latest rewards, there is still a month left for players to earn them.

    10 Jul 2024, 00:00

    LEAGUE of Legends’ latest season is coming to an end, and all the season’s rewards have now been revealed to players.

    Despite the announcement of the latest rewards, there is still a month left for players to earn them.

    The 2022 season officially ends on November 14, meaning there is still some time to reach the next tier of rewards.

    Rewards won’t be distributed until four weeks after the season ends, in the 12.22 and 12.23 patches.

    Here’s everything you need to know about League of Legends end-of-season 2022 rewards:

    League of Legends season 2022 ranked rewards

    If you place Gold or higher this season for either Solo/Duo or Ranked Flex, then you’ll receive a Victorious Sejuani skin. 

    More in League of Legends

    Each tier above Gold will receive a different special chroma for the skin, depending on the tier you placed in.

    Everyone who finishes in Ranked placements in either queue — or both — will receive an Eternals Sejuani Series 1 Permanent profile icon from your highest rank.

    You will also receive a Ranked profile banner trim.

    League of Legends season 2022 clash rewards

    The season for clash will also end on November 14, bringing with it clash-specific rewards.

    Most read in Gaming

    Rewards are based on the number of Victory Points you earned over the course of the season.

    However, everyone will receive a Clash Contender Icon no matter how many points you earned.

    Here are the rewards by your number of VP:

    • 100 -  Contender Summoner Icon
    • 400 -  Contender Clash Logo
    • 1000 - Contender Clash Banner
    • 2000 - Conqueror Summoner Icon
    • 3000 - Conqueror Clash Logo
    • 4000 - Conqueror Clash Banner
    • 5000 - Champion Summoner Icon
    • 6000 - Champion Clash Logo
    • 7000 - Champion Clash Banner

    Read More on The Sun

    League of Legends season 2022 honour rewards

    If you end the season at honor 5 or higher, you’ll obtain the Three Honours Malzahar skin.

    You will also receive the Malzahar Champion Permanent profile icon.
