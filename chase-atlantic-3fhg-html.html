    Chase Atlantic is an Australian alternative R&B band and production trio from Cairns, Australia, formed in 2014. The group consists of lead vocalist Mitchel Cave, rhythm guitarist and vocalist Christian Anthony, lead guitarist, saxophonist, and backing vocalist Clinton Cave, touring guitarist Patrick Wilde, and touring drummer Jesse Boyle. Chase Atlantic has released eight albums,"Dalliance" in 2014, "Nostalgia" in 2015, "Paradise EP" in 2016, "Part Three" in 2017, Chase Atlantic in 2017, Phases in 2019, "DON'T TRY THIS" in 2019, and Beauty in Death 2021.
