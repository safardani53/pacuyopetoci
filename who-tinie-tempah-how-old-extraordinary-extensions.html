    TINIE Tempah is the most successful British rap artist in UK chart history and the first to reach platinum sales status with his US debut single ‘Written in the Stars’.

    Here’s everything you need to know about the rapper and who he is.

    Who is Tinie Tempah and how old is he? 

    Patrick Chukwuemeka Okogwu is best known for his stage name TinieTempah.

    He is a 32-year-old English rapper and singer.

    He was born in London, and is the son of Igbo parents from Nigeria.

    Until he was twelve years old, he lived in South East London with his parents and three siblings: Kelly, Kelvin, and Marian.

    Regarding his London upbringing in his biography, Tinie states: "London is one of the only places in the world where you can live in a council block and see a beautiful semi-detached house across the street. Growing up around that was inspirational, it kept me motivated."

    Tinie's fame grew tremendously in 2010  when released his debut single ‘Pass Out’, which entered the UK Singles Chart at number one, and sold over 92,000 copies in its first week.

    How tall is Tinie Tempah and what’s his net worth?

    Tinie is 1.73 m tall.

    Spelling to The Guardian about his height, The British rapper said: "I'm 5 foot 9. Is that small? It's small compared to someone who's 6 foot 7".

    As of 2021, Tinie Tempah’s net worth is roughly $3 million.

    Who is Tinie Tempah's wife Eve De Haan?

    Eve De Haan is a 29-year-old artist from London.

    It is thought to believe that she is mixed race as her Instagram bio holds the English and Mauritian flag.

    She took a degree in Theology, which informed and influenced her work.

    The artist married Tinie in a surprise ceremony at a quiet Cotswolds church in 2019.

    Tinie has two children from his current marriage with Eve.

    The pair have a daughter, who was born in 2018, and another child born in 2021.
