    Get to Know '90 Day Fiance' Star Big Eds Daughter, Tiffany Brown

    Edward Allen Brown, famously known as Big Ed, got his fame from the TLC show 90 Day Fiancé. With his behavior towards girls and many allegations against him. He has managed to be one of the most controversial people in the 90 Day Fiancé franchise history.

    Before being a part of the franchise, Big Ed was married to Sandra Heckman. Their marriage didn’t last long since he cheated on his wife. The duo got divorced after only two years of marriage. They share a daughter — Tiffany Brown. 

    She Has Appeared In ‘90 Day: The Single Life’

    After a fiasco with his next girlfriend, Rosemarie Vega, Big Ed went on to date Liz. Their relationship is portrayed on 90 Day: The Single Life. This new relationship is under scrutiny since Liz is younger than her boyfriend's daughter Brown and bears a striking resemblance to her. 

    Because of the same reason, Brown also didn’t like how her 52-year-old father chose young girls to date. In the episode aired on April 24, Brown met up with Liz. She compared Big Ed’s ex-girlfriend Vega with Liz and told him how both girls were younger than her and had kids. 

    Brown was brutal in criticizing her father’s girlfriend. Liz was the first girlfriend she had ever met in person, which turned out to be unsuccessful and awkward. 

    She confessed to the cameras that even though Liz was younger than her, she still looked “rugged” due to Botox. Liz did not appreciate how Brown was treating her and made sure her boyfriend knew about it. 

    While it is fair for Brown to look after her father, many fans did not appreciate how she dragged another woman instead of talking to her father and calling him out.

    @thisisbiged 's daughter wow. She's calling Liz rugged like she's Victoria's Secret calibre or something lmao. Having concerns is fine, but there's no reason to attack appearance out of nowhere. It's a trashy thing to do. — Supreme Leader (@cantwak3up) April 27, 2021

    Brown’s Relationship With Her Father

    The father-daughter duo is pretty close. Big Ed adores his daughter and admits that she is just like him. He is very grateful for her and considers his 29-year-old to be the best thing in the world that he created.

    However, his decision to fly to the Philippines without informing her had caused a rift in their relationship. On season four of 90 Day Fiance: Before the 90 Days, Big Ed was in a relationship with Vega, a 23-year-old from the Philippines. After just three months of online dating, Big Ed flew from San Diego to the Philippines to meet his then-girlfriend. Adding to it, Big Ed had even sent over $5,000 worth of gifts to his ex-girlfriend during their short-lived relationship.

    Knowing that his new online girlfriend was six years younger than her didn't sit well with Brown. She eventually made a harsh decision and cut off all communication with her father.

    Regardless, the two have patched things up, and the pair are as close as ever. Just a month back, Ed took his daughter on vacation to Tulum Beach. Brown had posted the photos of the duo enjoying their time on his 55th birthday getaway.

    Brown’s Relationship With Her Mother

    Despite being a daughter of divorced parents, Brown is close with both of them. She often posts pictures with her mother on her Instagram, claiming how much she loves her in the captions. 

    She is also relatively close to her step-father. She considers him as a father and calls him “pops.” Recently, the two of them went to watch Vegas Golden Knights together. 

    Brown’s Profession 

    According to her Instagram bio, Brown is a fitness trainer and a real estate agent.

    She works as a trainer at Omalza Fitness in Las Vegas, Nevada. She frequently invites her followers to join her via her Instagram. As a real estate agent, she works at shareDOWNTOWN, a luxury apartment home. She is involved in the leasing & development of the apartment.
