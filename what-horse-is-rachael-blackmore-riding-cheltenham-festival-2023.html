    14 Jun 2024, 00:00

    RACHAEL BLACKMORE will be hoping her 2023 Cheltenham Festival is as triumphant as last year's.

    Back in 2022, Blackmore became the first-ever female jockey to win the Cheltenham Gold Cup.

    She rode A Plus Tard to Gold Cup victory at the 2022 Cheltenham Festival.

    A Plus Tard is trained by Irish horse National Hunt trainer Henry de Bromhead.

    Last year's winner is set to feature in the Gold Cup again at Cheltenham this year, and Rachael Blackmore is going to attempt back-to-back wins on A Plus Tard.

    What are the horses that Rachael Blackmore is riding at Cheltenham Festival 2023?

    The Irish jockey is set to ride a number of horses during the four-day horse racing festival.
