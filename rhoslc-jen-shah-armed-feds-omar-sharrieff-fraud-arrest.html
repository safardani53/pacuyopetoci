    REAL Housewives of Salt Lake City star Jen Shah's own home was swarmed by armed fed as the officers evacuated her sons Omar, 16, and Sharrieff Jr., 26, amid the star's fraud arrest.

    Just before agents of Homeland Security had swarmed in on the ladies of RHOSLC, Jen, 47, had made up a wild excuse about husband Sharrieff Sr.'s "internal bleeding" and barely escaped in the nick of time. 

    Previously, Jen nervously told her co-stars: “I have some bad news. I just got a phone call and [her husband] Sharrieff Sr. is in the hospital so I’ve got to go. 

    “I need to go there. He had an appointment and he has internal bleeding and they’re not sure if they’ll need surgery. 

    “I don’t know what’s going on. I don’t know what’s wrong. But you guys go.” 

    After Jen left, Whitney Rose, Heather Gay, Lisa Barlow, and Jennie Nguyen were all inside the bus when Homeland agents barged in on them.

    Homeland Security demanded answers and wanted to know where Jen was.

    The reality star ladies felt cramped inside the moving bus as they started questioning about how Jen made her money.

    After turning against each other and becoming suspicious, the group realized someone must have tipped off Jen and warned her that there was a S.W.A.T. team on its way. 

    THE RAID

    The group then discovered the news of Jen being wanted by the authorities for money laundering and telemarketing scheme on their cellphones. 

    They also learned that Jen was arrested while out on the road and could possibly receive up to 30 years in prison. 

    Meanwhile, at Park City, police cars and helicopters swarmed in on Jen's house and escorted her sons off the property. 

    While the armed feds were watching them closely, Omar and Sharrieff Jr. had their hands up as they walked out of their home.

    Afterwards, Jen headed out of the courthouse with her lawyer and went straight to her car while ignoring questions from reporters.

    After arriving at their location, the dinner talk with her co-stars was all about how they all felt "betrayed" over how Jen had lied to them and how she was on the run with stolen money. 

    THE TELEMARKETING SCHEME

    Back in March, Jen was arrested and charged with conspiracy to commit wire fraud in a "telemarketing scheme."

    In addition to Jen, police arrested her assistant, Stuart Smith - who was also featured on the Bravo show.

    They were each charged with one count of conspiracy to commit wire fraud in connection with telemarketing and one count of conspiracy to commit money laundering.

    According to legal documents, they were both accused of targeting senior citizens in order to scam them out of money in their alleged scheme.
