    WILLOW Tait's life at General Hospital has been anything but ordinary since being introduced in 2018.

    Due to Willow's recent storylines, fans are now wondering how much longer they have with Wiley Cooper-Jones' birth mom.

    Is Willow leaving General Hospital?

    A few weeks ago, Willow was given the shocking news that she was pregnant, but now she has an even scarier health problem to deal with.

    TJ Ashford, who is played by Tajh Bellow, not only told her that she is expecting, but he also explained that he believes she has leukemia.

    When asked why she wouldn't ask Michael Corinthos, who is played by Chad Duell, for help, she explained that she doesn't want to bother him.

    However, TJ has since urged her to seek help from anyone because the pregnancy is only going to make this worse.

    Many fans have since taken Willow's diagnosis as a sign that she will be leaving General Hospital, but at this time, it remains unclear.

    What have fans said about Willow's diagnosis?

    Shortly after the news of Willow's diagnosis was announced, fans quickly took to Twitter to discuss it.

    One user quickly shared their disagreement with GH's decision to give Willow a baby and said: "We could’ve gotten a Willow cancer storyline without making her have another baby."

    Another added: "It’s sad how isolated Willow is now. I thought when they had her become a nurse she’d get close to people like Elizabeth, Terry, Felix, TJ etc. Elizabeth comforted Willow at her son grave when she found out he died. They could’ve been good friends."

    A third said: "My girl Willow Leukemia? TJ is right, let's not carry this alone, girl. I know you have a tendency to do just that, as life has had you carry stuff alone long ago, but Michael will be there for you. His family is there too."

    How can I watch General Hospital?

    General Hospital is one of the longest-running soap operas in America, with its first episode dating back to 1963.

    Fans can watch the new drama unfold on weekdays on ABC starting at 2pm EST.

    It can also be streamed on Hulu, YouTube TV, and fuboTV with an active subscription.
