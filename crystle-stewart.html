    Early Life, Family & Education

    Crystle Stewart was born on September 20, 1981, in Houston, Texas, the United States of America, to father Robert Stewart and mother Cynthia Franklin Stewart.

    She was born under the birth sign Pisces. She has two siblings; a sister Breana Stewart and a brother, Robert Stewart. She is of American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity.

    She has completed her schooling at Elkins High School. She later attended the University of Houston from where she graduated in consumer science and merchandising.

    Modeling & Movie Career of  Miss USA Pageant 2008

    Crystle Stewart entered the entertainment industry as a model working for many advertisements and photoshoots.

    She first competed for the beauty pageant Miss Texas USA in 2002 and she became successful to be in the semi-final of the contest. Later in 2005, she became the third runner-up in the same competition.

    Crystle rose to fame after she won the Miss USA title in 2008. She had competed among 121 other contestants in Laredo, Texas.

    She hosted Miss Teen USA along with Seth Goldman in 2009 and later in 2016, she was the judge of the Miss USA pageant in Las Vegas, Nevada.

    Additionally, Crystle has also played the role of a secretary in alongside Thandie Newton, Tyler Perry, and Gabrielle Union. In 2018, she got her first primary role in the movie, Acrimony.
