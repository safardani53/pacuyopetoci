    Daniella Monet Harry Styles Girlfriend — 5 Things To Know About The ‘Victorious’ Actress

    1. She is a 1D fan!

    No surprise here! The stunning actress loves One Direction. On Sept. 13, she took to her Twitter to show her support for the boy-band as she watched them perform at the Rose Bowl! She tweeted, “Thoroughly enjoying the @onedirection concert right now.” Complete with the British flag emoji!

    2. She is a Nickelodeon darling!

    Daniella has worked on several different TV shows and movies for the network. Most notably, she played Trina Vega in Victorious. She also worked on the Fred movies and starred alongside of, Drake Bell in, A Fairly Odd Movie: Grow Up, Timmy Turner and A Fairly Odd Christmas. She is set to reprise her role as Tootie this year in the film, A Fairly Odd Summer.

    3. She started guest starring on huge shows at a young age

    Before hitting it big in 2011, Daniella had some guest starring roles on some pretty impressive shows. When she was just 8-years-old she landed her first gig on the show, Pacific Blue. After that she worked on The Bernie Mac Show, 8 Simple Rules and Zoey 101!

    4. Daniella supports PETA 

    The actress opened up to PETA about why she feels animal dissection is wrong. In the interview she opens up an incident in elementary school where she was asked to dissect a squid. She admits that she couldn’t do it and was actually sent to the nurse because she was crying.  In the video she also admits that she has been a vegetarian since she was just 5-year-old and has been vegan for the past 11-years.

    5. She is a huge advocate for a healthy lifestyle and loves to work out!

    Besides consuming a vegan diet, Daniella loves to workout — and she definitely has the body to prove it! On Sept. 6, the stunning actress posted a picture to her Instagram of her morning workout where she is lifting weights and working on her core while showing off her amazing stomach. On Aug. 23, she posted another shot where she is on vacation yet still manages to get in a good workout by running a 10k!
