    TWO teenagers are dead and a third fighting for life following a horror crash in West Yorkshire. A white Audi A3 is believed to have smashed into a telegraph pole after coming off the A6120 in Leeds at around 3.28am today.

    26 Aug 2024, 00:00

    TWO teenagers are dead and a third fighting for life following a horror crash in West Yorkshire.

    A white Audi A3 is believed to have smashed into a telegraph pole after coming off the A6120 in Leeds at around 3.28am today.

    An 18-year-old man and a 19-year-old woman were pronounced dead at the scene on Parkside Road, in Meanwood, north of the city.

    Another passenger, an 18-year-old man, was taken to hospital and remains in a critical condition following the early morning horror.

    A 22-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving on the westbound ring road.

    A scene and road closure are in place and officers from the Major Collision Enquiry Team are investigating the incident.

    Officers are now appealing for anyone who was driving in the area or lives along the route to check dashcam or home CCTV for any footage that could assist the investigation. 

    Anyone who witnessed the collision or who saw the white Audi A3 being driven in the area before the collision is asked to contact MCET officers via 101 quoting reference 13230541883.

    Or report online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat
