    26 Mar 2024, 00:00

    When it comes to sleep, comfort is queen — and in order to get comfy, you need to feel comfy in a pair of nice pajamas. “We sleep in our pajamas for hours on end, so it’s important to wear pajamas made from high-quality fabrics and ones that are comfortable and breathable,” says style expert Naina Singla.

    In addition to function, Singla says there is also a self-care component to pajamas. “A nice set of pajamas will make you feel great, will set the mood for after work, and helps you reset for the evening,” she explains, noting that a nice pair can also leave you feeling pampered, too. 

    Shopping for pajamas starts with pajama brands — and some are superior to others. With so many places to find nightgowns, sweatsuits, pants sets, and more, knowing where to find the best ones can feel a bit overwhelming. To mitigate that, we researched dozens of pajama brands to find the best options for a variety of budgets and styles.

    From Skims to Amazon to Sleeper, here are the best pajama brands of 2024, including the brand recently donned by Jennifer Garner herself.

    Stars Above

    Our Top Pick: Beautifully Soft Short Sleeve Notch Collar Top and Shorts Pajama Set

    Target is our go-to for many trendy and classic clothing items alike — including pajamas. Particularly, the retailer's Stars Above brand has so many different styles to choose from, both online and in-store, with new styles released frequently. It’s a fantastic place to find everything from classic silhouettes to budget-friendly dupes of designer PJs to trendy sets and more.

    In addition to pajamas, Stars Above is also the holy grail for some of the best loungewear pieces, too, including plush robes, slippers, and sweatsuits. On top of that, it has one of the best return policies, allowing you to return your order online or in-store within three months of purchasing.

    Size Range: XS-4X | Shipping Policy: Free shipping over $35 or with a Target Red Card | Returns/Exchange Policy: Within 90 days of purchase

    Amazon Essentials

    Our Top Pick: Snug-Fit Cotton Pajama Set

    Amazon is an excellent place to shop for pajamas and the Amazon Essentials brand has some of our favorite options. Whether you’re shopping for night dress, matching pajama sets, or trendy ensembles, the brand offers a variety of styles at a range of budget-friendly price points, too.

    On top of that, it has one of the best shipping policies around, thanks to Amazon Prime, and the brand carries sizes XS to 6XL, making it a more size-inclusive pajama option in relation to other brands.

    Size Range: XXS-6XL | Shipping Policy: Free Prime shipping | Returns/Exchange Policy: Within 30 days of purchase

    Lake Pajamas

    Our Top Pick: Pima Long Weekend Set

    Lake Pajamas were born out of a dream to marry classic style and modern function; cofounders Cassandra Cannon and Anne Read Lattimore wanted to create a brand of sleepwear that would be chic enough to wear while hosting a holiday party. (Case in point: The DreamKnit Kimono Pajama Set and the Poplin Piped Pajamas are about as chic as they come as far as PJs are concerned.)

    A brand worn by Jennifer Garner, Lake Pajamas offers a variety of prints, colors, materials, styles, and categories to choose from — it even has mens and kids versions so the whole family can match. We particularly love the holiday prints that feature novel illustrations and designs that are completely unique to the brand.

    Size Range: XXS-XXL | Shipping Policy: Free shipping on orders over $50 | Returns/Exchange Policy: Full refund within 30 days (sale items eligible for store credit only)

    Aerie

    Our Top Pick: Flannel Ruffle Boxer

    As one of our go-to brands for loungewear, Aerie also knocks it out of the park when it comes to pajamas. We love this brand because we can always count on them to have some of the softest and most comfortable materials, we well as chic and trendy designs, too.

    From the brand's waffle knit wrap top to its adorable flannel pajama sets, it offers styles we can’t get enough of, especially for the holiday season. Plus, it has lots of fun accessories, such as matching pajama headbands, comfy Minnetonka Chesney Scuff Slipper, and our favorite Kitsch The Pillow Satin Sleep Mask.

    Sleeper

From its crowd-favorite feather trimmed sets to its classic linen looks, the pajama brand creates some of the most beautiful sets that are so chic you’ll want to wear them out of the house — and you totally could with options like the Lurex Lounge Suit with Pants or the White Rabbit Shiny Pants.     How to Pick the Right Place to Buy Pajamas

    Return Policy

    Whether you want pajamas for women, kids, or the family, you should choose a brand with a good return policy when shopping online so that you have enough time to try on the garments and decide whether or not you need to return or exchange them. For the most part, many brands offer a 30-day return policy; however, some might offer less or more than that. 

    Comfort

    When picking a pajama brand, Jennifer Michalski-Bray, a celebrity fashion stylist, says comfort is key. “You have a comfy bed, comfy pillow, comfy blankets, and you need to ensure you don’t miss a trick by not wearing comfy pajamas,” she notes. Michalski-Bray recommends looking for brands that make pajamas in natural fabrics — think cotton and linen — which ensures comfort and breathability, especially in the warmer summer months. 

    Versatility 

    Shopping at a pajama brand that boasts plenty of versatility in its designs also matters, especially if you want to mix and match pajama sets and get more use out of them, says Singla. She recommends choosing a brand with a color palette that pairs well together so you can easily mix and match pieces.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What is the best brand of pajamas?

      You can’t go wrong with any of the pajama brands on our top list. However, each one has its own unique loungewear and pajama offerings. For an eco-friendly brand that boasts a luxe feel, consider shopping for pajamas at Pact. If you prefer more versatility in style and budget, Target is always a good place to start. Skims is a go-to for its neutral, comfy designs, while P.J. Salvage offers lots of fun color and print variety. We also love Jambys for a more loungewear approach to pajamas.

    • Which pajamas are most comfortable?

      When it comes to the most comfortable pajamas, Singla says it really depends on your personal preferences, “but silk or cotton will be most comfortable in terms of fabrics.” To determine which pajamas are most comfortable, Singla says to consider your environment, as this will help you decide which fabric choice is most appropriate.

      She also recommends looking at the overall size and fit of the pajamas. “In general, pajamas that are not too tight and not too loose will be most comfortable,” she explains.

    • What is the coolest pajama material?

      If you’re shopping for cooling pajamas, Sapna Palep, the co-CEO of Journelle, says to “look for organic cotton for the cool lightweight feel.” She notes that this material is great, especially in the summer, but it can also keep you comfortable during the winter months, too. “Organic cotton is grown without harmful chemicals and is allergen-free and non-irritating on your skin,” she explains, adding that it’s also super soft and lightweight.

    • What are the most breathable pajamas?

      For a pair of pajamas with supreme breathability, Palep recommends modal. “Modal is often blended with other fibers like cotton, which adds durability and softness,” she explains. Palep also notes that modal is a great pick for pajamas because “the weave of the fabric is very breathable and absorbent.”
