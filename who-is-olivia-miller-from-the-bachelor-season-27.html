    The Bachelor returns to television on Monday with a freshly eligible Bachelor and 30 suitors. Olivia Miller, a 25-year-old stylist from Ohio, is one of the ladies slated to participate in the program and compete for Zach Shallcross’ heart.

    According to ABC, she is:

    “Olivia M., who was born and reared in a small town with a loving family, is seeking the love of her life! Olivia may be a city girl today, but she will always remain a small-town girl at heart. She studied fashion and has a strong interest in style and design. Prepare your outfit, Zach!”

    The Bachelor season 27 will begin on ABC on Monday, January 23, at 8 p.m. ET.

    Meet Olivia Miller, who will participate in The Bachelor season 27.

    The next season will have 30 single ladies competing for the title of Final Rose. Olivia Miller, a 25-year-old stylist from Ohio, is one of the ladies confirmed to feature in season 27. She is a LIM College graduate with a degree in fashion merchandising and styling. The season 27 cast member was named National American Miss Jr. Pre-Teen. She also won three additional state championships, including Ohio Princess Jr. Pre-Teen and Pre-Teen in 2003.

    National American Miss said in 2015 that her greatest memory of the year when she won the national crown in 2013, was going to other state pageants and meeting other participants, which gave her an opportunity to meet other NAM state directors.

    It went on to say:

    “Another pleasure was attending the Miss America contest in Las Vegas. Her favorite part of the year was spending extra special bonding time with her mother, Chris. Traveling from state to state, she made up her introductions on the way to the pageants.”

    The small-town girl enjoys reading and binge-watching television series. Despite the fact that she now lives in a huge metropolis, she was reared in a small town and will “always remain a small-town girl at heart.” The stylist likes going to new locations and doing hot yoga. According to ABC, she can imitate the sound of a dolphin. Aly, Anastasia, Ariel Bailey, Rebecca, Brianna, Brooklyn, Cara, Catherine, Charity, Christina, Davia, Gabriella, Genevie, Holland, Jessica, Kaitlyn, Kimberly, Kylee, Lekha Ravi, Madison, Mercedes, Olivia L., Olivia M., Sonia Sharma, Vanessa, Victoria J., and Viktoria E. join her on The Bachelor season 27.

    The Bachelor season 27 will be hosted by Jesse Palmer.

    The primary cast member of the ABC program will be Zach Shallcross. He previously competed on The Bachelorette season 19 but quickly departed when he felt his connection with Rachel wasn’t strong enough. Jesse Palmer, who will host the following season, revealed his involvement in The Bachelor season 27 during the season finale of season 19. She was a bachelor in 2004 and will help the future cast through the process.

    Jesse had this to say about the show:

    “Falling in love is one of life’s greatest blessings, and I am honored by the chance to return to the program as host this season to provide wisdom garnered from direct experience to the newest Bachelor, and I am pleased to play a little role in his journey.”
