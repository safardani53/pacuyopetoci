    The Real Housewives of Atlanta Season 9 Episode 7 Recap: Model Behavior

    02 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Phaedra and Kandi hoped to put the past behind them yesterday evening on The Real Housewives of Atlanta Season 9 Episode 7.

    A new deluge of rumors emerged, however, and made them question everything … or at least whether a detente would really be possible.

    Porsha Williams decided the best way to get Todd to meet her family was to wine and dine him, so that’s exactly what she did to get somewhere with the relationship. 

    However, the dinner did not go all that well when it became clear that Todd was pretty nervous about the whole thing.

    In fact, he stayed quiet throughout the whole dinner. 

    Porsha’s sister seemed somewhat concerned, but she had good reasons for it.

    She wanted to make sure his intentions were good because he already had a family with kids. 

    Todd made it clear to Porsha’s sister that he is very interested in Porsha and wants to make it work with her.

    Porsha looked so delighted to see her man speak up for her. Might this pair actually manage to stay a thing? 

    We’ll need to stay tuned to find out. 

    If you watch The Real Housewives of Atlanta online, you probably already know that things have been tense between Kenya Moore and Matt Jordan. 

    Watch The Real Housewives of Atlanta Season 9 Episode 7 Online

    They just can’t seem to stay out of trouble, but that did not mean Kenya was about to give up on Matt.

    Instead, she reeled her father in to get his two cents on what she should do to save the relationship. 

    “I feel like he’s such a great person, but that whole other side when he gets mad and he starts throwing stuff — something has got to give,” she sighed.

    Regardless of Kenya’s love for Matt, her father was not impressed in the slightest at Matt’s actions and was worried about her safety.

    Seeing the broken glass panels on the garage door definitely made her father think the worst. 

    When Kenya set up a meeting between her dad and Matt, Matt kept texting that he would not be attending, but eventually decided it was best. 

    Matt took issue with Kenya for using him to fix things around the house, but Kenya was quick to reveal that she only cared about him fixing the things that he broke. 

    As Matt got ready to run off, Kenya’s dad told him to get it together if he thought the relationship could actually work.

    Matt took this on board and disappeared. 

    Elsewhere, Phaedra and Kandi went to dinner, but it was no civil dinner. Phaedra was annoyed about the comments Mama Joyce made about her, so she took her frustrations out on Kandi. 

    For what it’s worth, Kandi defended her mother. The comments her mother made were grim, but Kandi was not about to admit that. 

    Kandi then got personal, saying that Phaedra was already seeing people before Apollo went to jail.

    After bringing NeNe Leakes into the argument, Kandi got up to leave because she was convinced Phaedra was talking trash.
