    Admirers of the Aquaman actor were met with confusion as 'Jason Momoa without beard' began trending on social media.

    Why is 'Jason Momoa without beard' trending?

    In November 2022, 'Jason Momoa without beard' started trending across Twitter and other social platforms.

    Although the Dune star did not actually shave his beard, doctored images of his hairless face went viral.

    As users noticed these false pictures of Jason, netizens tweeted their mixed reactions to the photos.

    Some users even compared beardless Jason to Cheers actress Kirstie Alley, as well as politicians Sarah Huckabee Sanders and Steve Bannon.

    "Why am i attracted more to Jason Momoa without his beard?! Gosh, my man did put an impression on me. Clean chin = more attractive," tweeted a third admirer.

    When did Jason shave his beard?

    The last time Jason appeared to shave his beard was for a movie role.

    From March 2019 to July of that same year, Jason sported a freshly trimmed face for his part as Duncan Idaho in Denis Villeneuve's Dune.

    Regarding the decision, the father-of-three told Newsweek: "That's one of the first things Denis asked me.

    "I think he felt a little nervous asking me that. But it just felt like, no one's ever asked me to do that."

    He added: "He was like 'I see Duncan as this' and he had very good reasons why and I said 'yeah, I think it's smart.'

    "I just had to grow it back for Aquaman 2 and for the other TV show I'm on, [titled] See.

    "I just thought it kind of made sense to shave it, grow it back and we had time to do that. So it made sense. I did that for him. There are not many people I would do that for."
