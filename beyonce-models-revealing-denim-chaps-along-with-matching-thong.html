    BEYONCE brought a whole new meaning to a full moon in her latest fashion photoshoot.

    The Irreplaceable singer wore a pair of very revealing denim chaps along with a matching thong, blue T-shirt and cowboy hat as she promoted her Ivy Park Rodeo collection with Adidas.

    Her Bootylicious new snaps have certainly caused a stir. And while I’m sure the new range will sell out in moments, I have a feeling these chaps won’t be at the top of most people’s shopping list.

    We’ll probably see some desperate reality TV wannabes trying to rock this look on next year’s Love Island.

    But I reckon if you wore these on a trip to the supermarket, you might get chucked out for indecent exposure.
