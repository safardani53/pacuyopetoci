    IT'S official - the season of spending evening after evening snuggled up on the sofa is finally upon us. And if you ask us, it couldn't have come sooner.

    And no winter evening would be complete without a selection of cosy knitted blankets to tuck yourself up in - and fortunately for us, savvy shoppers have tracked down the perfect, purse-friendly ones.

    Posting on the Facebook group We Love Mrs Hinch, one shopper posted an image of a £30 chunky knitted blanket with the caption: "Spotted these in Primark yesterday."

    What's more, the dreamy throw also comes in a Mrs Hinch-approved shade of grey.

    But while other users gushed over how cosy the blanket looked, others said it was too pricey for Primark before suggesting cheaper dupes online.

    One replied: "I must be tight but I think £30 for a Primark throw is a bit steep. I got a very nice one very similar from Ikea for £15."

    Where IKEA's £15 throw is more tightly woven that the Primark throw, the £16 eBay offering is almost identical and comes in pink, grey, white and teal.
