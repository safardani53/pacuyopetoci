    On February 2, Young Buck was arrested and jailed for allegedly damaging his ex-girlfriend’s car. Cops said the rapper’s ex reported a domestic disturbance on Wednesday, according to an arrest affidavit. The artist had texted her earlier that day to say he was coming over, but she had no idea what he said when he began kicking at her front door.

    Buck’s exclaimed that when she went outside after he left, she found her car smashed up in her driveway, with damage to the driver’s and passenger’s sides. The incident was also captured on video, and police confirmed that her vehicle had been damaged.

    The reason behind Young Buck’s arrest

    After causing damage to his ex-girlfriend’s car, Young Buck was arrested for vandalism. Because the incident was captured on video, the charges were also proven.

    While he was driving, the cops tracked him down and pulled him over to arrest him. He was arrested and booked into a Nashville, Tennessee jail before being released on a $1,000 bond.

    In the last few years, Young Buck has had his fair share of legal issues. In October 2020, he was arrested on charges of domestic assault, unlawful possession of a weapon, and vandalism over $10,000.

    In December 2019, the 40-year-old was arrested on a felony fugitive from a justice warrant. Despite the fact that the charges were not made public, the warrant was issued in Georgia, and Buck was forced to remain in custody until his court date because he was not eligible for a bond.

    Buck was sentenced to prison in July 2016 after allegedly threatening to burn down his ex-house girlfriend while on probation for gun charges in 2012. In 2017, he was charged with domestic assault and vandalism for the second time.

    After allegedly stabbing a man who had punched Dr. Dre at the Vibe Hip-Hop Awards in California in 2004, the Nashville, Tennessee native was charged with assault with a deadly weapon.

    For a long time, he had an on-again, off-again relationship with 50 Cent. They weren’t getting along because of rumors that Buck was dating a transgender woman.
