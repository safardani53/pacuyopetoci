    Holy romance! Taylor Swift and Tom Hiddleston continued their lovefest overseas while touring the Vatican in Rome on Wednesday, June 29.

    Swift, 26, and the British actor, 35, coordinated in white as they walked around. They held hands and kept to themselves while others appeared to stop and take photos of them.

    Earlier in the week, the lovebirds took pics of each other outside the Colosseum. The “Style” singer wore a yellow dress and comfortable blue flats, perfect for walking and exploring.

    The new couple have been in Italy since Monday, June 27. Since their arrival, they’ve have been busy sightseeing, dining at romantic restaurants and spending time at one of the city’s lavish five-star hotels.

    Swift and the I Saw the Light star, who have already met each other’s parents, have been racking up frequent flier miles over the past couple of weeks. They have spent time in Rhode Island, Nashville, L.A. and visited Hiddleston’s mother, Diana, in England.

    As previously reported, Swift and Hiddleston were first caught kissing in Rhode Island on June 14, weeks after Us Weekly confirmed that Swift and Scottish DJ Calvin Harris had ended their nearly 15-month relationship. Hiddleston most recently dated his Avengers costar Elizabeth Olsen.

    “Tom said he hasn’t found the right woman yet,” an insider tells Us Weekly in the latest issue. “But he thinks Taylor is the girl he has been searching for.”

    For more on Swift and Hiddleston’s getaway, pick up the new issue of Us Weekly, on stands now.
