    PLANNING your next summer expedition? The best hiking shorts for women will keep you cool and comfy as you explore the great outdoors.

    A good pair of walking shorts is an essential on a long hike, whether your trail is taking you on an established route or off the beaten track.

    Can I wear hiking boots with shorts?

    Absolutely! You can wear hiking boots with hiking, climbing and walking shorts.

    We recommend you also wear a pair of thick, sweat-resistant and comfortable socks though - suitable for your outdoor adventures.

    What should I look for in hiking shorts?

    Hiking shorts should be versatile to suit all of your outdoor needs. As a rule of thumb they should be sweat and water-resistant, as well as super-comfortable and lightweight to chuck in your backpack.

    They come in a variety of lengths and materials, suitable for all kinds of activities, from cycling and trail-running to climbing and walking.

    Are women's hiking shorts waterproof?

    Not necessarily: like any garment, features vary.

    Most hiking shorts are made from moisture-wicking and breathable fabrics, as you'll of course be building up a sweat as you walk and climb and will want something to keep you cool and dry.
