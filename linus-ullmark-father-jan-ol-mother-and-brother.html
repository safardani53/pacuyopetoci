    In May 2014, Ullmark was selected by the Buffalo Sabres to a three-year, entry-level contract. Buffalo Sabres chose Linus in the sixth round as the 163rd overall pick in the 2012 NHL Entry Draft.

    He was returned on a loan to Modo Hockey of the Swedish Hockey League (SHL) for the 2014-15 season.

    Ullmark spent three seasons playing with the Modo before being recalled to the Sabres. He made his NHL debut in October 2015.

    Moreover, his remarkable performance led the team towards multiple victories, in the match against Arizona Coyotes and Carter Hutton.

    Afterward, in July 2021, Ullmark left the Sabres and signed a four-year contract with the Boston Bruins. There he plays alongside David Pastrnak, Charles Mcavoy, Hampus Lindholm & Brad Marchand, to name a few.
