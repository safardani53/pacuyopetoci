    Putting those style skills to work, work, work! Rihanna is collaborating with Manolo Blahnik on a globally-inspired shoe collection called “Denim Desserts,” the label announced on Friday, March 4.

    PHOTOS: Rihanna’s Red Carpet Style Evolution

    The “Work” singer, 28, and the beloved shoe brand (most famously adored by Carrie Bradshaw) are launching six limited-edition designs that “incorporate the inimitable style of Manolo Blahnik with a mix of Rihanna’s quirky and artistic influence.” That means denim, sequins galore and embroidery inspired by Rihanna’s hand tattoos.

    And the pop-R&B singer, for one, can’t wait until they finally land. “The day I see a woman in the street wearing my shoes,” Rihanna began in a statement, “I am sorry for that woman because I’m going to literally run after her, shouting, ‘Stop! Selfie! Who are you? Where did you get them?’ I’m going to have a moment!”

    PHOTOS: Editors’ Winter 2016 Style Picks

    While the Barbadian superstar is surely proud of the entire collection, she admitted that she does have a favorite: the 9 to 5 boot. “Those boots are dangerous!” she said in the release. “If you’re trying to get back home to your bed, don’t wear them boots! You will get kidnapped in those boots, those boots are trouble.”
