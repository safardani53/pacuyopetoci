    BARGAIN Hunt is the staple of lunchtime viewing with millions of loyal fans.

    The BBC One show has a whole team of experts, and they are joined by host Danny Sebastian.

    Who is Danny Sebastian?

    Danny Sebastian is an antiques expert who first appeared on Bargain Hunt in 2017.

    It was then revealed in 2022 that he was the latest presenter to join the show.

    On joining the presenting line-up, Sebastian told the BBC at the time: "I’m absolutely over the moon, it’s beautiful really.

    "It’s like a dream come true. I always thought I’d love to do this job – I’m so extremely fortunate to be given the opportunity.

    Read more on Bargain Hunt

    Sebastian moved into the antiquing world at a point when he was ready for a career move, he said: "I was very fortunate and worked with a big celebrity player in the antique game, I then saw an auction house full of mid-20th century gear and instantly knew antiques was for me," he said.

    "It’s a fabulous show, very iconic and presenting it has given me a real kick."

    What TV shows has Danny Sebastian starred in?

    Outside of Bargain Hunt, Sebastian has appeared on Celebrity Eggheads, Flipping Profit, Celebrity Mastermind and Susan Calman's Antiques Adventure.

    Danny also fronts CBeebies’ Junk Rescue on BBC, which is a children's game show which combines traditional crafts and making sustainability fun.

    He has also appeared on BBC One's Dealing with Dickinson, and Put Your Money Where Your Mouth Is.

    Did Danny Sebastian have a heart attack?

    In 2019 Danny revealed in a tweet that he had clogged arteries but was on mend.

    It is not reported that he had a heart attack, but he did say he was "lucky" after a health scare.

    At the time Danny tweeted: "I thought I was #fit, I looked it, I felt it, I believed it, but internally I wasn’t. Our arteries can get clogged up create problems+even death.

    "We must #eat #healthy #exercise #sleep plenty & #stress little & NEVER #smoke.

    "I’m #lucky but not all of us are. Look after yourself."

    Is Danny Sebastian married?

    Danny is in a long-term relationship with a woman called Joanne but little else is known about her.

    It isn't believed they are married but they have been together for many years.

    According to the Express, Danny and Joanne live together in his"vintage emporium home in Wellingborough" with their three daughters.
