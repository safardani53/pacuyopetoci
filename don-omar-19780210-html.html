    William Omar Landrón Rivera, known by his stage name Don Omar, is a reggaeton artist, record producer and Puerto Rican actor. He is sometimes referred to as El Rey and King of Kings. On September 1, 2017, he announced that he would retire from a series of concerts at the Coliseum José Miguel Agrelot in Puerto Rico, scheduled for December 15, 16 and 17. He returned to music on April 20, 2019 with the song "Single Ramayama" starring Farruko.
