    Whaaaat. 

    Everyone’s jumping to conclusions and wants a reunion to happen.  Even Chris Brown is offering sage advice to Bieber on how to win back the love of his life.

    “Chris is sitting back, looking at Justin’s antics and shaking his head," a source reportedly told Hollywood Life.

    "For starters, Justin is acting like a younger Chris. Arrogant, womanizing, breaking hearts and acting like his sh** don’t stink. Chris wants JB to know that ain’t cool and that he’d better stop juggling these girls like he’s at a circus before he loses the love of his life.”

    Totes.

    “Chris had to learn the hard way and he only wishes he never played Rihanna or Karrueche like he did. It’s obvious to Chris that Biebs and Selena belong together, and he only hopes JB realizes that before she walks out of his life forever."

    It’s not confirmed whether or not Gomez went to Bieber’s LA concert on March 21st, but eye witnesses inside the venue did see his hook-up buddy, Hailey Baldwin.

    “[Justin] has 100% invited [Selena] and wants her to attend his show,” Hollywood Life claimed days before the concert.

    She was probably busy washing her hair/rearranging her sock drawer/watching Netflix.
