    Karrueche Tran has opened up for the first time about the demise of her relationship with Chris Brown and the bombshell we all assumed caused it.

    Looks like we all pretty much assumed correctly.

    Many believed that lats week’s Karrueche Tran bloody lip photo may or may not have been her attempt at trolling Team Breezy, but she says no way.

    She wants nothing to do with him, period.

    “I make it so we don’t have to cross paths so it’s not awkward,” Tran explained of her attempts to make her life 100 percent free of Chris Brown.

    “But I do get on Instagram and there’s people tagging us, or I’ll hear a song or something,” Karrueche admits. “I kind of just have to block it out.”

    As for moving on romantically?

    “I am 100 percent single,” the model told Real 92.3 LA radio. “There are a few dudes trying to make their pass. I’m really just trying to focus on me.”

    We know Tyson Beckford and Karrueche hung out, and Chris was far from happy about that, so clearly there’s still something between these two.

    A history, anyway. Not a future.

    When Tran heard that Chris Brown is a father for the first time in March, it was after we’d heard about it … and his little Royalty just turned one.

    In other words, he knocked up Nia Guzman and did not mention this fact.

    “Yeah, when it hit TMZ, I was like ‘Oh, okay. Ugh,'” Tran recalled of that little secret of his. “I called him. I cussed him out, and I was like [I’m done].”

    “You know, ’cause after the whole Rihanna thing, it’s been like a year … we’ve been trying to fix what was broken. That was the icing on the cake.”

    Speaking of the “Rihanna thing,” Kae says she has no ill will toward the superstar, despite their much-documented love triangle involving Chris.

    “We just dated the same guy, and that’s kind of what it is,” Tran reflects.

    “It’s always a competition thing. They compare us, but she is Rihanna.”

    “She’s a pop star and she’s beautiful and she makes great music, and I’m just me and I’m growing and working into myself. You can’t compare us.”
