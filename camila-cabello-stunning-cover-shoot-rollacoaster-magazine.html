    29 Sep 2024, 00:00

    CAMILA Cabello looks incredible on the cover of the latest edition of Rollacoaster magazine.

    The Cuban singer, 20, stares sultrily at the camera in a satin bronze crop top and a quirky pair of flares in one memorable snap from the shoot.

    On the cover she lies over a bed in a white and pink striped minidress while raising her feet into the hair complete with a pair of heels.

    She then moves to a chair outdoors in the sunshine, resting her heeled foot on a table and tilting her head backwards with her eyes closed.

    Another shot shows her gazing out of a window in a stylish, frilled vintage dress.

    In her interview with the magazine, Camila opens up about her debut solo album The Hurting. The Healing. The Loving. due for release later this year.

    She explained: “I think I was just avoiding talking about the hurt that I was feeling and I didn’t want to come to terms with it, and I feel like writing is such an intimate process, I mean at least for me, there’s no way I could write about stuff without going through it at the time or feeling a certain way about it at the time – it leads to a lot of self-discovery because you’re asking yourself questions, like how do I feel today? How do I feel about this?”

    Camila left her former group Fifth Harmony nine months ago and discussed how the writing process differs now she's a solo artist.

    “The biggest challenge I think, honestly, the workload, because I’m such a perfectionist and I want everything to be right and I stress over every little detail – it all requires 100 per cent,” she said.

    “In a group, other people do it for you, if that makes sense, because nobody can handle five different opinions on one thing.

    Meanwhile during Fifth Harmony's performance at the VMAs on Sunday a silhouette of a fifth dancer was projected onto screens behind them, standing between Normani Kordei and Lauren Jauregui.

    But as the song changed pace, the shape was thrown backwards off stage and disappeared.

    The stunt is believed to have been a subtle dig at Camila, who is said to have exited the group without warning any of her bandmates and it sparked a public feud between the stars.

    They have released a new self-titled album, their first without Camila, but The Sun has exclusively revealed that they are on the verge of a split.
