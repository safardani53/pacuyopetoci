    Zoraida Sambolin is a renowned American television journalist who is married to executive vice president of the Chicago White Sox, Kenny Williams. This amazing couple tied the knot in . Despite being married for two amazing years, Sambolin and Williams still look like they just met yesterday and fell in love. This married life is going well and there is no rumor of divorce between this perfect pair.

    Zoraida Sambolin and Kenny Williams’ affair

    Sambolin started dating Williams in 2010 after Williams' return from spring training in Arizona. According to Sambolin, this pair met at one of their friend, Ozzie’s Salsa and Stars event in Chicago. After that, they dated for years before taking the big step in their life.

    Sambolin and Williams got engaged on 2012’s Christmas Eve after Williams proposed Sambolin. Sambolin recalls the whole incident and says: “Kenny asked me to marry him on Christmas Eve of 2012. We had a house full of family and he pulled me into his office and got down on one knee…and then asked me to help him up. Of course, I said yes. The next day we went to Holy Name Cathedral and he asked me again in a very romantic, very intimate setting as he had originally planned.”

    When Williams is asked about his proposal, he says that he was really nervous that day and it did not go according to his plan. He says: “I was excited all day and pulled her aside after dinner and asked her in my office at home. She knows I can’t save a surprise for long. But the next day, we went to the church, kneeled before the altar and I asked again the way I had intended — she pretended to be surprised.”

    Zoraida Sambolin Married Kenny Williams in 2014

    Sambolin and Williams waited for few years before tying the knot. They got married in July 2014 among friends and family.
