    Primark launches Peter Rabbit pjs from 6... and they look perfect for Beatrix Potter fans

    ATTENTION Beatrix Potter fans! Primark has launched some adorable pyjamas based on her most famous character. The high street chain has released cute Peter Rabbit sleepwear featuring the mischievous cartoon bunny.

    11 May 2024, 00:00

    ATTENTION Beatrix Potter fans! Primark has launched some adorable pyjamas based on her most famous character.

    The high street chain has released cute Peter Rabbit sleepwear featuring the mischievous cartoon bunny.

    The cute pyjamas are perfect for those keeping an eye on the pennies, as the top costs just £6 and the leggings are £8.

    They feature Peter Rabbit exploring London, with the London Eye, Big Ben, St Paul’s Cathedral and the Gherkin in the background.

    The white t-shirt top says “I love London” and “Peter Rabbit”, while the grey leggings come with a sweet blue bow and are covered in cartoons of Peter.

    Bunny lovers can get hold of the pjs in UK stores, and shoppers can’t wait to get their hands on them.

    One shopper gushed on Instagram: “Yes!!! Looks so cute!”

    Meanwhile another said: “I’ll defo have my eyes peeled for them.”

    The Peter Rabbit range comes at a good time, as the brand new film reboot will hit UK cinemas on March 16, 2018.

    In the CGI film, Peter’s feud with Mr. McGregor escalates to greater heights than ever before as their fight to gain control of McGregor’s coveted vegetable garden and earn the affections of the warm-hearted animal lover who lives next door.

    As their adventure continues, Peter and his pals find themselves travelling to the Lake District and London in their quest.

    Comedy legend James Corden will be voicing cheeky Peter, with Domhnall Gleeson as formidable farmer Mr McGregor, Margot Robbie as Flopsy, Daisy Ridley as Cotton-Tail and Sia as Mrs Tiggy-Winkle.

    It's not the first clothing item that Primark has released based on a movie or TV show.

    The chain also sold Rugrats sleepwear and socks from £2.50, and has transformed their Tottenham Court Road store into a spellbinding Hogwarts haven.
