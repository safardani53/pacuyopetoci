    What is Quavo's net worth and what's his real name?

    American rapper, songwriter and musician, Quavo, has an estimated net worth of $15million.

    Born on April 2, 1991, at Atlanta in Georgia, as Quavious Marshall, the rapper is best known by his stage name Quavo.

    He is a member of the hip hop trio Migos, which includes Offset and Takeoff.

    Two of their albums - Culture and Culture II - topped the charts in 2017 and 2018 respectively.

    They also claimed the No1 spot in 2016 with their single Bad and Boujee featuring Lil Uzi Vert.

    Quavo is also one half of rap duo Huncho Jack with superstar rapper Travis Scott.

    His own studio album Quavo Huncho reached No2 in the charts in 2018.

    How tall is Quavo?

    Fans are often wondering how tall Quavo really is.

    We can reveal that he is 5ft 10in.

    Who has Quavo dated?

    Quavo started dating Saweetie in 2018.

    However, they kept their relationsip secret for a while until Saweetie confirmed that they were an item during an appearance on talk show The Real.

    But it wasn't until March 2019 that they made relationship official on Instagram, around the same time she released her music video titled emotional, which featured Quavo.

    But in March 2021 it was reported that fans believed they had split.
