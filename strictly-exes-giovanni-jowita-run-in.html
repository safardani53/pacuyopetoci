    STRICTLY's Giovanni Pernice and Jowita Przystal had an awkward looking run in as they arrived at rehearsals.

    The pair, who fell for one another during the last series of the BBC dancing show, chose to end their relationship.

    The Sun revealed in December that Sicilian dancer Giovanni and Polish pro Jowita, both 32, had fallen for each other during filming.

    Jive specialist Giovanni made a move on Jowita as they spent time together backstage.

    Jowita, who went on to win the competition with wildlife cameraman and presenter Hamza Yassin, was whisked off her feet by Giovanni.

    However, there appears to be no love lost as they bumped into each other at rehearsals for this year's Strictly.

    Jowita Przystal was seen cosying up with her rumoured boyfriend Arduino Bertoncello as the rehearsals kicked off.

    She came face-to-face with Giovanni as she left with Arduino with both men also appearing to have stern words with each other.

    Jowita was snapped as she seemed to calm the situation down before laughing it all off with Arduino.

    Jowita, who joined Strictly in 2021, was previously in a relationship with pro partner Michael Danilczuk, 31.

    This week's rehearsals also saw the Strictly Come Dancing class of 2023 meet up for the first time.

    Zara McDermott looked as though she was raring to go as she rocked up in a tiny pair of shorts and trainers, while former EastEnders hunk Nigel Harman arrived on a motorbike.

    Strictly Come Dancing returns to BBC One next month with Claudia Winkleman and Tess Daly back on hosting duties.
