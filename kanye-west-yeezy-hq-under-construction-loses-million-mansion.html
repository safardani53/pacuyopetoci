    KANYE West's office has been undergoing some major changes.

    The rap music star also recently put his $50million home in Malibu, California on the market - and is set to "lose millions."

    Kanye's, 46, abandoned headquarters for his fashion franchise, Yeezy, is getting remodeled.

    In new pics, the building - which is located in Los Angeles - is being completely transformed as the interior has been completely gutted.

    The photographs capture the messy rooms that have tarps, trash, and tons of wood scattered throughout.

    With all of the construction happening, the Flashing Lights rapper could be taking his empire in a new direction.

    Following his multiple controversies, Yeezy was dropped by several companies.

    Adidas revealed it was terminating its business dealings with the star last year.

    He was also dropped by the likes of Balenciaga and GAP and claimed he lost "$2 billion in a day."

    This month, it was reported Adidas is considering writing off the remaining $320 million worth of Yeezy shoes left unsold for the third release since cutting ties with the star.

    Meanwhile, the father of four put his abandoned beachfront house on the market for $4million less than what he paid for in 2021.

    MOVING ON

    Months after purchasing the home, the rapper teamed up with award-winning architect Tadao Ando, to turn the property into his dream home to share with his children: North, 10, Saint, eight, Chicago, five, and Psalm West, four.

    Inside the luxe California home is completely concrete.

    However, Kanye completely renovated the place, got rid of the entire interior, and has now been left empty.

    The rooms are abandoned without any sort of finishing to protect the foundation.

    The star has decided to leave the home as is and put it on the market, asking for about $4million less than what he paid.

    "It’s priced to reflect the need for the interior finishes to be replaced. On the positive, it can be brought up to 2024 standards, it was built about 10 years ago," real estate agent Jason Oppenheim said.

    Jason - of the Oppenheim Group - is the agent in charge of selling the home.

    He continued: "The architectural integrity of Tadao Ando’s work exists today. He’s largely known for his concrete work and the significant cost of this house is in the concrete work."

    PAYING NO MIND

    While his office is undergoing major changes as well as his home, Kanye has been spending time away with his wife, Bianca Censori, 28.

    Last week, the couple - who married in 2022 - were spotted leaving the Four Seasons Hotel in Las Vegas, Nevada after one of Kanye's raves was shut down by the police.

    In paparazzi pics, Kanye wore his familiar all-black ensemble including dark sweatpants and packed on the layers with sweatshirts.

    Kanye pulled the look together with black boots.

    Next to him, Bianca wore a white spaghetti-strapped bodysuit and tan suede boots.

    In her arms was a vast white oversized furry blanket.

    Bianca positioned the blanket so it was shielding most of her stomach.

    The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed on Friday that the star claimed that he wanted another baby into the mix with Bianca.

    He rapped about having a fifth kid in a new song that debuted at a chaotic listening party in Las Vegas on Thursday night.

    KANYE'S NEWEST RANT
