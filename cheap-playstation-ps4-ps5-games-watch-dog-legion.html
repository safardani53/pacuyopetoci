    15 Mar 2024, 00:00

    PLAYSTATION fans have just two days to get a popular Watch Dogs franchise game worth £59.99 for under a tenner.

    The 2020 title, Watch Dogs: Legion, has undergone a whopping 85 per cent price cut in the Sony PlayStation Store.

    The game is now worth just £8.99, but only until midnight on 5 January.

    Watch Dogs: Legion is a Ubisoft open-world action-adventure that lets players explore London in the not-so-distant future as the city faces its 'downfall'.

    The PEGI 18 title explores an oppressive surveillance state, private military control of the streets, and a powerful crime syndicate that preys on the vulnerable.

    It's mostly a one player game, but online play with up to eight online players is supported with a PS Plus subscription.

    READ MORE ON PLAYSTATION

    Watch Dogs: Legion has secured an overall rating of five stars on the PlayStation Store.

    Meanwhile, on Metacritic - a site where both players and critics can review games - it has snagged a 76 approval rating.

    Unlike other games in the Watch Dog franchise, Legion let's you play as virtually anyone you see in the open-world city, in a unique twist.

    According to ZTGD's critic review on Metacritic: "Anyone entering Watch Dogs: Legion looking for a by the books open world game is going to find a lot to enjoy.

    "There are small puzzles to solve, plenty of collectables, lots of missions and I would say the shooting and driving are the best this series has ever been.

    "Fans of the series like myself, however, will be disappointed by the lack of enjoyable characters as well as stand out missions and story beats. "

    ImpulseGamer added: "Personally, I’m glad that Legion took a darker turn and really explored the true possibilities of how technology can be used for malice purposes, as it gives the player a real sense of triumph taking down these digital villains.

    "I do recommend caution for younger gamers, as 'Legion' is definitely aimed at an older audience.

    "Regardless, Watch Dogs: Legion has an enormous amount of replay value and a very compelling storyline with unexpected twists along the way."

    Several fans of the franchise also noted that Legion failed to take the throne as the top title out of the Watch Dog franchise.

    However, most still enjoyed the game, saying that it's worth it at a reduced price.
