    HAMPERS are a lovely festive family treat. 

    Although you can buy some readily prepared hampers in stores. 

    It’s great to have fun building your own. 

    One mum of two has shared the Christmas hamper she built.

    Muddlethroughmummy is an account dedicated to sharing tips and tricks for positive parenting from a mum of two. 

    In a recent video the account user shared the festive hamper she put together for her mum all from Aldi.

    Read more Christmas stories

    She said: “Putting together an Aldi Christmas hamper.”

    She continued: "They actually did have hamper kits in here but I’m going to save money by using the one I have bought with me”. 

    If you are after a hamper kit there £5.49 in Aldi.

    First the mum of two adds Aldi organic prosecco which costs £7.99.

    Most read in Fabulous

    She said: “This organic prosecco is delicious.”

    Next Aldi’s Women’s Eau de Parfum No.1, they are £6.49.

    The perfume scent is lime, basil and mandarin but you can find other scents for the same price. 

    The account user said: “I actually use this Eau de parfum, I think it’s great value.” 

    A box of luxury blond chocolate Florentines for £2.49.

    She said: “I’ve seen these in M&S”. 

    A salted caramel hot chocolate bomb which is £1.69 this also comes in other flavours. 

    The mum of two said: “For me Christmas socks are a must although they only had them in size 12 - 2 which I thought was a little bit odd”.

    But she still added a pack of those.

    Next a gel inclusion candle for £3.99 which is a dupe for the White company Christmas candle.

    The candle is scented with spiced orange, frosted berries and frankincense with myrrh.

    The mum of two finished by saying: “Here is my hamper let me know who you would gift this too”.

    The total cost of this hamper was £25.14.
