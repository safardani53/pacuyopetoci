    TEEN Mom alum Gary Shirley has posted "scary" photos of his damaged Indiana property post-tornado on Instagram.

    Fans requested that the MTV personality "be safe" after he shared several snapshots of the damage on Monday.

    Gary, 36, captioned the post: "How’s your day? How’s mine you ask? Well. Spent some time in my basement (tornado) and then Heard big crash and a bang and wallah, we all safe."

    The photos captured images of an uprooted tree, broken and scattered fence posts, and what appeared to be the remains of a work shed.

    In the post's comment section, one fan wrote: "I’m glad you are all safe."

    Another person commented: "Super scary I am glad you are all okay, that’s what’s important."
