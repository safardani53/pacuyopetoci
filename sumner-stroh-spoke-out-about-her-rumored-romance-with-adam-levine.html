    27 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Instagram model Amber Rose says singer Adam Levine, 43, wanted to name his third kid after her after they had an affair. Social media star Summer Stroh, 23, posted a video on TikTok on Monday, September 20, in which she showed a snapshot of a chat she claims to have had with the lead singer of Maroon 5. Her video description read:

    I feel ashamed that I ever got connected with a guy who lacked both regret and respect.

    She said in the video that they had an affair that lasted a whole calendar year. Over 11 million people have seen the video, and there are over 44,000 comments. Behati Prinsloo, a model for Victoria’s Secret, is Adam Levine’s wife as of 2014. So far, Behati has carried three healthy children for the couple.

    What Sumner Stroh said about her rumored romance with Adam Levine

    Summer Stroh, a model from Texas, has over 355,000 Instagram followers. At the beginning of the video, the model said:

    As in, “I was having an affair with a guy who is married to Victoria’s Secret supermodel.”

    She continued, saying that she felt “exploited” by the musician since she was “young and innocent” at the time of the romance. She blamed her “easiness to be influenced” on being a newcomer to Los Angeles at the time.

    Sumner said that she and Adam Levine had been dating for a year until they suddenly stopped talking to each other “over a period of months,” and she posted a screenshot of their discussion to Instagram with the caption “I miss you.” She showed off a Direct Message from the Payphone artist asking for her permission to give her name to his third kid.

    In her words, Summer remarked

    “My integrity was unwittingly breached. The whole thing was a sham to get me to do what they wanted.”

    A friend of hers sold the screenshots to a tabloid, she said, adding that she had never wanted to handle the problem in “such a public way.” She continued her explanation by saying:

    “As a professional Instagram model, I am well aware of the repercussions of my work. Being connected to this narrative, I am aware of the generalizations it contains.”

    Sumner responded to a negative remark with another TikTok video in which she said:

    “It’s common knowledge that he has a wife of ten years. His family is the only ones hurt here.”

    She stated she was “ashamed” and “ashamed” of herself for becoming involved with the singer, but said she was new to Los Angeles at the time and “under the notion” that Adam Levine’s marriage to Behati was ended.

    After that, she said:

    “I thought they were keeping it under wraps to prevent bad publicity.”

    She said she didn’t have any reason to suspect foul play since she was talking to Adam Levine’s verified account, and she also admitted that she wasn’t the victim. She said she was sorry and that Behati and her children were the “true victims” of the situation. As of now, the musician and his camp have not issued a comment.

    All About Sumner Stroh

    Austin, Texas native Summer Stroh, 23, is a prominent figure in the world of social media. After graduating from New Braunfels High School, she enrolled at UT. She completed her degree in advertising and marketing.

    There is one older sister and two younger brothers in her family. Baylen Stroh, Summer’s older sister, also has a large following on Instagram and runs a successful jewelry company. Sponsorships and product endorsement partnerships have helped boost Summer’s estimated net worth to $600,000.
