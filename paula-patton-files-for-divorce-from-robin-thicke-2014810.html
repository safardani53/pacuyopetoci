    Paula Patton has filed for divorce from estranged husband Robin Thicke, the Los Angeles Superior Court confirms to Us Weekly.

    Patton and Thicke, who were high school sweethearts married for eight years, separated in Feb. 2014. At the time, a source close to the "Blurred Lines" singer told Us it had "been a long time coming" and that "their marriage was unraveling for a long time."

    The split came after Thicke's controversial 2013 VMA performance with Miley Cyrus and after he was rumored to have cheated on Patton after pictures surfaced of him grabbing another woman's bare butt while posing for a photo.

    According to TMZ, Patton cited irreconcilable differences and is seeking joint legal and physical custody of their 4-year-old son Julian. TMZ reports that the divorce is "harmonious."
