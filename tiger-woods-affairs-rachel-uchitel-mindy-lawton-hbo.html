    TIGER Woods has had a string of high-profile affairs over the years with Rachel Uchitel, Mindy Lawton, porn star Joslyn James and more.

    The new HBO Max documentary "Tiger" sheds light on the past scandals and revisits some of the golfers alleged elicit trysts with a slew of different women.

    After the scandal erupted during his marriage to model Elin Nordegren, The Sun revealed that Tiger lost $100 million in a divorce settlement.

    Since then, the athlete has struggled to win a single tournament and struggled to repair his tarnished reputation as 13 women claimed they had x-rated liaisons with him.

    In light of the revealing new documentary, here is a selection of ladies he made swoon over the years – including adult actresses, a cocktail waitress, and a Playboy model.

    Rachel Uchitel

    Rachel Uchitel featured in the documentary, saying she's "not a home-wrecker," that "made a mistake," and revealed that Tiger called their romps "plugging in."

    She also claimed that she had a "love addiction" during their affair 10 years ago, which prompted her to ask Dr Drew for help, and she's reportedly writing a tell-all book.

    Uchitel's grandfather owned famous nightclub El Morocco in the 1960s, loved by famous names like JFK and Cary Grant.

    She became the face of 9/11 when a photo of her clutching an image of her missing fiance Andy O'Grady went viral.

    Tiger ex-mistress worked as a hostess at the Tao nightclub in Las Vegas.

    In 2009, The National Enquirer broke the story that they were together in Melbourne, where Woods competed in the Australian Masters.

    Mindy Lawton

    Waitress Mindy Lawton, 34, revealed that Tiger is "very well endowed."

    She said he "knows his way around the bedroom" and that "on a scale of ten, [she] would give him 12," according to the Huffington Post."

    According to Mindy, Tiger loved sleeping with her in various locations – car parks or in a garage – anywhere but the bedroom.

    In 2011, she was sentenced to a year in jail for a 2009 drunk-driving crash, according to court records cited by the Sun Sentinel.

    Joslyn James

    Porn star Joslyn James claims she romped with Tiger who sent her explicit texts outlining what he'd like to do.

    James – whose real name is Veronica Siwik – also alleged that she fell pregnant twice during their rendezvous.

    She was reportedly on a "Most Wanted" list after owing $20,000 in child support in Washington in 2019.

    James is still making adult movies in California, according to the Bleacher Report, and began filming TV series VIP last year.

    Loredana Jolie

    The Sicilian-born TV star and Playboy model waxed lyrical about sex with Tiger in a tell-all book "The Real Diary: Lessons from the Good Time Girl to Champion."

    In it, she revealed the “largely endowed” sportsman loved threesomes with her between 2006-2008.

    She also said that Tiger “loved kissing” and “always used to convince me to send him sexy text messages and picture messages.”

    Loredana boasted that Tiger was "well-endowed" and enjoyed  "girl-on-girl" action and encounters with men in her bombshell memoir.

    Kalika Moquin

    Las Vegas marketing manager Kalika was the third mistress to be named in 2010 – she told a US magazine that Tiger confessed he loved her.

     Life & Style reported that she visited Woods' Las Vegas hotel room during the weekend of October 23, 2009 more than once.

    The Bleacher Report revealed that she now markets pools.

    Jamie Jungers

    Las Vegas lingerie model Jamie claimed she had an 18-month affair with Tiger.

    Jamie was working as a promotions girl who entertained high-rollers in Las Vegas’s top clubs when she first met Tiger in 2005.

    She comforted him when his dad Earl died in 2006 but the Daily Mail alleged that she took pictures of him naked and threatened to sell them if they broke up.

    After their affair became public knowledge, Jamie said she drank liters of vodka a day and took dozens of prescription pills to cope with abuse from angry Tiger fans and trolls who threatened to throw acid in her face.

    In 2018 reality TV star Dog the Bounty Hunter allegedly rescued her from a sex and drugs den in Vegas where she had been kept as a sex slave.

    Cori Rist

    Sex with Tiger was “passionate” and like “fireworks” said Manhattan socialite Cori Rist on their alleged tryst.

    She also said Tiger enjoyed post-coital Fruit Loops and cartoons.

    She's reportedly in the middle of divorcing nightclub promoter Neil Santos and has a seven-year-old son.

    Theresa Rogers

    Theresa Rogers, 48, reportedly hooked up with Tiger before he got married and the fling continued afterwards.

    Hers was the longest of all Tiger’s affairs, lasting five years.

    She was dubbed the "cougar" who, according to sources, "taught him everything he needed to know to be a great lover."

    Holly Sampson

    A renowned porn star, Holly called her 45-minute one-night of passion with the sports superstar after his bachelor party as an “amazing” experience.

    She had previously appeared on homespun TV comedy The Wonder Years before turning to porn.

    She was hired as entertainment for a surprise for Woods and brought along two friends, one blond and one brunette.

    In a video for porn company Naughty America, Holly said their encounter was "amazing" and that he has "beautiful everything," and "he was really good."

    Emma Rotherham

    The secret British lover, Emma, 42, was reportedly paid £300,000 in hush money to keep quiet about their affair.

    They reportedly met at the Blue Martini in Orlando, Florida, before having "awesome sex" in his office on the couch.

    In 2010, the News of the World reported that he “loved her to dress in black stockings and suspenders.”

    They supposedly enjoyed pre-golf-practice sex at least once a week – some of which occurred while Elin was pregnant – and he once flew Emma out to a tournament in Michigan for “mind-blowing” Ambien-fuelled sex.

    Devon James

    Tiger romped with another porn star Devon James in 2009, who alleged that Tiger paid her for sex when she dabbled in prostitution.

    James claimed she gave birth to Tiger's love child and is reportedly trying to sell a steamy video of their encounter.

    Her own mother, Sandra Brinling, called her a "pathological liar" and claimed she knows exactly who her child's real dad is, reported WLTX.

    Raychel Coudriet

    Tiger reportedly romped with his neighbors 21-year-old daughter for two hours.

    Julie Postle

    A cocktail waitress from Orlando, Florida, Tiger’s 11 mistress claimed that he texted other woman while they were in bed together.

    Her ex-boyfriend Brian Kimbrough alleged that when Woods would call her cellphone, his name was "Mr. Brightside" an apparent reference to The Killers' song.

    Jaimee Grubbs

    Californian waitress Jaimee alleged that she had a 31-month affair with Tiger, who had a penchant for sexting her.

    The then 24-year-old cocktail waitress told Usmagazine.com that she met the golfer at a nightclub in Las Vegas two months before his wife gave birth to their first child.

    Grubbs claims to have hundreds of message exchanges from Woods, who once described himself as her "boyfriend" in a string of texts cited by the New York Post.

    Woods reportedly once left a message on Grubbs' voice mail urging her to remove her name from her mailbox greeting.

    She appeared in Maxim in 2010.

    Lindsey Vonn

    Olympic skiing champ Lindsey dated Tiger for three years before calling it quits in May, 2015.

    She opened up about her romance, likening it “living in a fishbowl."

    Vonn and Woods were one of the hottest couples in the sporting world during their relationship.

    She has revealed since she found it “really hard” being with the golfing legend due to all the scrutiny they were under as a celebrity couple who were both at the peaks of their respective sports.

    The couple had met at a charity event in 2012.

    She retired as the most successful female skier of all time - where she recorded a staggering 82 World Cup victories throughout her career.

    Erica Herman

    Current girlfriend Erica met Tiger when she became general manager of his pop-up restaurant, The Woods Jupiter, in February 2017.

    A friend said at the time: “Tiger has had such a turbulent year, what with his problems with the police and his back surgery, but Erica has stood by him all the way.

    “They get on incredibly well and she is not put off by his past troubles with women.”

    She was also by his side two days later in a Florida court when he pleaded guilty to reckless driving after being found drugged to the eyeballs on painkillers.

    Erica grew up in a modest rented bungalow in the Florida town of Boynton Beach.

    Elin Nordegren

    Elin was working as a nanny for Swedish golfer Jesper Parnevik in 2001 when she met Tiger at The Open in Lytham.

    They tied the knot in 2004 in Barbados but it all went south in 2009 when Tiger's many affairs became common knowledge.

    She divorced him, and got around $136million.

    Elin, 40, and her NFL star boyfriend Jordan Cameron, 31, filed to change their son’s name from Filip Nordegren Cameron to Arthur Nordegren Cameron on December 5, 2019.

    The Sun confirmed a judge approved the name change for the baby boy, who was born on October 4, 2019.
