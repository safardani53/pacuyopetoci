    KATY Perry has shown off her curves in a see-through lace dress at the American Idol after-party.

    Katy revealed her black leather thong underneath the sheer dress, which she teamed with black lace-up heels.

    The American Idol judge finished off her look with a chunky black ring and matching earrings.

    She styled her hair in glossy waves and framed her features with shimmery eyeshadow and taupe lipstick.

    Katy made an outfit change for the after-party, having worn a shimmering orange cut-out dress for the finale hours earlier.
