    Who pays for bridesmaid dresses?

    You'll find each wedding is different, and depending on the budget that some brides will pay for their bridesmaid dresses, some may contribute, and some may ask the bridesmaid to buy their dresses.

    Each bride is to their own!

    Where to buy bridesmaid dresses?

    There are a number of places you can buy bridesmaid dresses on the high street.

    We're currently loving ASOS, Oasis, Pretty Lavish and New Look.

    The great thing about these high street options is they tend to be a bit cheaper too!

    Where to hire bridesmaid dresses?

    Don't want to pay for a bridesmaid dress you'll only wear once? Why not rent it?!

    You can rent dresses from By Rotation, and Hirestreet here.

    Do bridesmaid dresses have to match wedding colours?

    Not necessarily, but many brides choose to.

    Some brides will tie their bridesmaid dresses into the flowers, some will choose a seasonal shade and others may go for white - very chic.

    How to choose bridesmaid dresses?

    There are a number of things to take into account when choosing your bridesmaid dresses, but the main thing is that you and your bridesmaids are happy.

    Think of your bridesmaid's individual styles, and look you'd like to create.

    It may be that you want to mix and match, or you may want an in sync look.
