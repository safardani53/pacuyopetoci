    A DIY whizz has shared a hack for getting the corners of your rug to lay flat and it's a total game changer.

    Sara, who often shared organisation and home tips on her Instagram recently left viewers amazed with small but mighty hack.

    If you've got any rugs in your home your probably already know how annoying it can be to try and get those pesky corners to lay flush against to floor.

    There are a few different ways you can try and stop the corners from curling, but this one won't cost you a single penny.

    All you need to do is take a few ice cubes from your freezer and lay them on top of the corner in question - or all of them if they're all being unruly.

    Then grab something heavy to hold to very edge in place as the ice melts into the fabric.

    READ MORE ON DIY

    Once the ice has melted you can remove the weight and your rug should be totally flat.

    Sara said: "I saw this somewhere and wanted to give it a try, I must say I'm pleasantly surprised."

    That said, the mum wasn't sure how long the trick would last, but it's quick and easy enough to repeat every so often if the corners do pop back up.

    It turns out the ice trick is good for other rug and carpet problems too.

    One viewer commented: "Ice also fixes carpet divots you get when you move your furniture."

    Other viewers loved the simple trick too and couldn't wait to try it in their own homes.

    "Omg I'm off to make some ice," one raved.

    Another commented: "I saw this the other day and tried it with a rug in my classroom today and was in shock that it worked.

    "I was ready to get rid of it ice for the win!!"

    And someone else wrote: "You can just wet it with cold water too, I always use this on carpets with 'puppy ear' ends."
