Capriati has established several the youngest records in history at the start. of her career. She made her professional debut in 1990 at the age of 13 years and 11 months, reaching the hard-core tournament final in Boca Raton, Florida. Capriati reached the semi-finals of Roland Garros on his debut and then became the youngest player to reach the top 10 at the age of 14, 235 days in October of the same year. After losing in the first round at the 1993 US Open, Capriati took a 14-month hiatus from professional tennis. His personal struggles during this time have been fully documented. In 1998, Capriati won his first Grand Slam singles match in 5 years at Wimbledon.
