    Asda has launched a Disney pyjama range for adults for 15 with all the favourite characters

    31 May 2024, 00:00

    WINTER is coming, which is the perfect excuse to get into cosy pyjamas and snuggle under the duvet with a movie.

    And this year is set to be even more magical as George at Asda has launched its own women's adult Disney pyjamas, featuring characters like Bambi and Aurora.

    The incredible collection proves that kids don’t have to have all the fun – and thankfully they aren’t too expensive either.

    The Disney sleepwear range costs £15 and includes a variety of long-sleeved tops, leggings, full-length trousers, t-shirts and jumpers.

    Choose from all your favourite characters, including the Princess Aurora, Minnie Mouse, Eeyore and Bambi and Thumper.

    The packages could even be the perfect Christmas present for Disney-loving friends and some come wrapped in a pretty bow, ready to be unwrapped.

    There is even a Minnie Mouse three-piece dressing gown and pyjamas set for £22, so you can really look the whole part this winter.

    CAPTAIN HOOKED! Primark launches new Disney slippers with prices starting at £3

    They aren’t the only Christmas items at Asda which are getting shoppers excited.

    The budget supermarket is also selling a cheese advent calendar – and it costs just £8.

    George at Asda is also selling a £18 red jumper dress which is a dead ringer for a £1,295 designer version.

    Meanwhile Primark has launched a Harry Potter Christmas range with glittering baubles – and it looks absolutely magical.
