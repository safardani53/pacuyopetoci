    When is Black Friday 2021?

    Black Friday is the sales bonanza that falls on the last Friday in November.

    It's the day after Thanksgiving, the fourth Thursday of the month - so it will be the 26 November this year.

    Black Friday deals often start trickling through a few days and even weeks ahead of the big event.

    We'll be updating you on the best Black Friday Christmas Tree deals here so stay tuned...

    When is Cyber Monday 2021?

    Just after Black Friday, Cyber Monday arrives on November 29 this year.

    Be ready to catch some more Christmas tree discounts and deals when it comes around.

    How much does a Christmas tree cost?

    Christmas tree prices vary greatly - you can find a basic artificial tree for under £10, all the way up to £100+ if you're looking for an all-singing, all-dancing, pre-lit, 7-foot tree with baubles.

    Real Christmas trees - Nordman firs - typically start at around £45 for a 4-foot tree.
