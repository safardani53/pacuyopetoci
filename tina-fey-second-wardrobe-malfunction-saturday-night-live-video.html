    Tina Fey Suffers Second (Hilarious) Wardrobe Malfunction

    In a new promo, Tina pairs up with Kenan Thompson, who couldn’t be happier that she’s returning to host SNL.

    After Tina announces that she’s hosting, Kenan adds in, “There will be no more wardrobe malfunctions like there were at the Emmys … I mean, unless you want to do one. I think it would be funny.”

    Tina crosses her arms, and continues to say “No, Kenan. It’s not going to happen again.”

    Then, as she unfolds her arms and puts them up to say, “It’s under control,” her blazer opens revealing that she’s wearing — nothing! Thats right, both breasts are out!

    “I forgot my shirt,” she says super quite and seriously, folding her arms. The only thing more funny than that is the reaction on Kenan’s face.
