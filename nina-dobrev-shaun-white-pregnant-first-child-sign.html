    NINA Dobrev has fans convinced she's expecting her first child with her boyfriend, Shaun White, based on a major clue in new photos.

    The actress and the Olympic gold medalist went public with their relationship in May 2020.

    Fans have since followed their sweet love story, and many think they spotted a sign that they're expanding their family.

    Last week, Shaun, 37, shared photos and videos on Instagram of him and Nina, 34, attending the wedding of Jesse Buss, co-owner and assistant general manager of the Lakers, and his longtime love Sarah Jordan.

    Shaun sported a black tux while Nina wore a body-hugging floral off-the-shoulder dress, with her brunette hair flowing straight down.

    One pic showed the couple huddled close and striking a silly pose at the camera while at the venue.

    "Wedding crashers," Shaun jokingly wrote in his caption and tagged the bride and groom.

    "I thought I saw a baby bump on the 3rd slide," one person commented.

    SWEET LOVE STORY

    Fans have been shipping Nina and Shaun since they made their relationship official via Instagram.

    Nina shared a funny photo of the twosome, with her holding scissors and him with a frightened expression on his face.

    It appeared that Shaun finally realized how famous his girlfriend was, which he admitted he wasn't initially aware of Nina's TV star status.

    “I actually didn’t know anything about her,” Shaun told People when talking about their relationship in January 2022.

    He then confessed that he realized Nina was a big name when others asked for her picture and not his at the event where they met in 2019.

    "Can we get a photo...with her?" Shaun recalled the waiters asking him that night.

    "And I was like, 'What's happening? What's going on?' It was actually really funny," the athlete laughed.

    WEDDING BELLS

    Their connection has since gotten stronger, with Nina supporting Shaun when he competed in the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing.

    Nina also moved into Shaun's Los Angeles home, making many wonder if they'll tie the knot soon.

    “They are so freaking cute and solid together,” a source told Page Six. “I would not be surprised if an engagement happens any day now.”

    “They share so many interests, they’re both driven, they’re both into self-healing and well-being. They’re both very spiritual and motivated," they continued.
