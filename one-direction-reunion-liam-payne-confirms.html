    Liam Payne reveals One Direction stars in secret talks to celebrate 10 year anniversary with special

    LIAM Payne has confirmed One Direction are planning a special project together to mark their tenth anniversary in July. The singer revealed he is in constant contact with bandmates Harry Styles, Niall Horan and Louis Tomlinson about plans to mark the milestone.

    06 Jun 2024, 00:00

    LIAM Payne has confirmed One Direction are planning a special project together to mark their tenth anniversary in July.

    The singer revealed he is in constant contact with bandmates Harry Styles, Niall Horan and Louis Tomlinson about plans to mark the milestone.

    However he didn’t say whether former bandmate Zayn Malik, who quit in 2015, would be involved.

    In an exclusive interview with The Sun’s Bizarre column to celebrate the release of his new single Midnight with Alesso, he said: “We've got a ten-year anniversary coming up so we've all been speaking together a lot over the last few weeks which has been really nice.

    “To hear a lot of people's voices and seeing old content and different things that we haven't seen for a long time or never seen before, it's very interesting.

    “At the moment I'm not sure what I'm allowed to say.

    “There's a number of different things that we are all working on to try and make happen and people are forwarding emails around.

    “But more than anything it's just been a real good time for us to connect together again.”

    The group, who sold more than 50million records, went on an indefinite hiatus four years ago but he loves what his bandmates have done in the meantime - especially Harry.

    Louis Tomlinson says it’s ‘inevitable’ that One Direction will reunite

    Liam said of the group’s success: “It's insane, it really is.

    “I still can't believe, I still feel like I don't know what the hell I'm doing.

    “It's amazing and everyone's had such great success. It's been great hearing a lot of Harry's stuff at the moment.

    “Adore You, I see it in the charts all the time. I think he's really found his sound at the moment which is a joy to watch.”
