    SISTER Wives’ Janelle Brown liked Christine's new photo, where she looked much thinner than before.

    However, the black sheep of the wives, Meri, snubbed her sister wives' post by ignoring it completely.

    Christine, 48, took to Instagram to share a series of photos of three of her daughters as they spent a couple of days at the ski resort, Arizona Snowbowl.

    Along with the sweet pictures, she also shared a snap her daughters had taken of her where she looked much slimmer.

    "IN LOVE WITH THIS SUNSET!," the Sister Wives star shared.

    "We’re chillin’ at the #flagstaffsnowbowl tonight. Really chillin! It’s cold!!"

    Surprisingly, second wife Janelle praised the mom of six in the comments and showed her support.

    "This is really completely great in every way," the 51-year-old wrote.

    She topped it off with heart emojis.

    On the other hand, Meri ignored the post as she didn't like or comment on it and Robyn has stayed MIA on social media.

    Christine hasn't been the only one who has been rocking a much slimmer figure as both Janelle and Meri have also lost weight amid the coronavirus pandemic.

    Just last week, Janelle posted that after years of being insecure, she finally feels “confident” in a bathing suit.

    She captioned a selfie while at the beach: “For years I made excuses about going anywhere that required swimsuit.

    "All of my insecurities... As I became more accepting of my wonderful body and all the things it could do I became more confident.

    “I’m a work in progress for sure but I missed out on so much fun through the years. No more! So here is my yearly post about how to go to the beach. Put on your suit and go.”

    On her Facebook page, she often posts healthy, farm-to-table recipes including kale salad, cauliflower mash, baked cabbage and more.

    She has also offered her health care services to fans.

    As for Meri, 49, she too has been showing off her trim figure.

    She posted a TikTok video working out at the gym.

    The LuLaRoe clothing seller also revealed she has been working with a personal trainer.

    Meri has even cut her hair and has been wearing more makeup for a full-body transformation.

    The new looks come as fans suspected Kody, 52, was in coronavirus quarantine with his “favorite” wife Robyn, 41, and their five children.

    While Kody has tweeted from time to time, Robyn has been completely silent, as even her jewelry business, My Sisterwife’s Closet, has been on hold.

    Kody reunited with his wives only a handful of times since the New Year.
