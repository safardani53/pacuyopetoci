    SHE has previously been labelled 'Britain's most beautiful woman' by one of her fans.

    And she has set pulses racing again after posting two stunning snaps on Instagram for her followers to wow over.

    Former Celtic and Scotland defender Jack Hendry has been with model Robyn Emma Keen for several years after meeting her during his spell with the Hoops.

    The model, who has worked for the likes of Boohoo and EGO, now lives in Belgium with the defender after he opted for a new life in Europe following his Parkhead exit in 2021.

    The centre-back left Celtic for Oostende on an initial loan before sealing a very short permanent switch as Belgian giants Club Brugge then came calling shortly after.

    Hendry has established himself as a well-known defender from his exploits in Scotland and in Europe as he boasts a following of 80,000 on Instagram.

    But Robyn isn't too far behind him as she has 30,000 of her own.

    And the Scotland wag has stunned fans yet again after posting the two glamorous pics for all of her followers to see.

    It's not the first time as one fan labelled her "the most beautiful British woman" in a recent post she uploaded, where she teased her new red-haired look.

    She's now wowed her followers yet again, this time with a photo of her in a busty corset top.
