    FOOTBALL fans are even more convinced Kylian Mbappe is moving to Real Madrid after a picture emerged of him packing up his stuff.

    The Paris Saint-Germain forward is out of contract at the end of the season and looks set to leave the Ligue 1 champions on a free.

    Real Madrid have been the favourites to land his signature for a long time - with Mbappe nearly moving to the Bernabeu last summer.

    But now a deal appears to be closer than ever and his artist friend Ora Ito hinted that the Frenchman was already moving his stuff elsewhere.

    A leaked Instagram photo showed Mbappe's stuff in boxes and he was tagged in the image alongside a praying emoji.

    Back in January, it was reported that the World Cup winner had agreed an £800,000-a-week deal with Real Madrid.

    Mbappe has made no secret of his desire to join Real Madrid.

    When discussing his desire to leave, he told RMC in October: "I asked to leave, because from the moment where I did not want to extend, I wanted for the club to receive a transfer fee so that they could bring in a quality replacement.

    “This club has given me a lot, I have always been happy [during] the four years I have had here, and I still am.

    "I said it early enough so that the club could react.
