    Robyn then got surprisingly nasty in the clip when looking back instead of moving forward: "He's had wives reject him affection-wise because they are uncomfortable or they don't like it.

    "If you're sitting there just constantly nagging at your husband about what you didn't get even though you have this apple in your hand, but you really want her orange... how is he supposed to feel appreciated?"
